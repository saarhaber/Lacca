Brown Buick Allures at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Buick Allure in our paint database was in 2007, with one brown paint shade named Antique Bronze.

The last offering of Brown on a Buick Allure in our paint database was in 2010, with 2 shades of brown paint named Gold Mist and Magna Steel.

In our database we found 7 offerings of Brown in 4 of the 4 production years of the Allure (2007-2010). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on an Allure in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
