Blue Buick Envisions at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Buick Envision in our paint database was in 2020, with one blue paint shade named Dark Moon Blue.

The latest offering of Blue on a Buick Envision is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of blue paint named Quartz Blue.

In our database we found 6 offerings of Blue in 6 of the 6 production years of the Envision (2020-2023 and 2025-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on an Envision in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
