Blue Buick Envistas at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Buick Envista in our paint database was in 2024, with one blue paint shade named Ocean Blue.

The latest offering of Blue on a Buick Envista is available now in the 2026 model year, with 2 shades of blue paint named Aquamarine Blue and Ocean Blue.

In our database we found 4 offerings of Blue in 3 of the 3 production years of the Envista (2024-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
