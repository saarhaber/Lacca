Red Buick Encores at PaintRef.com


The first Red Buick Encore in our paint database was in 2013, with one red paint shade named Ruby Red.

The latest offering of Red on a Buick Encore is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of red paint named Brilliant Red.

In our database we found 22 offerings of Red in 14 of the 14 production years of the Encore (2013-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
