Red Buick Cascadas at PaintRef.com


The first Red Buick Cascada in our paint database was in 2017, with one red paint shade named Sport Red.

The last offering of Red on a Buick Cascada in our paint database was in 2019, with 2 shades of red paint named Rioja Red and Sport Red.

In our database we found 5 offerings of Red in 3 of the 3 production years of the Cascada (2017-2019). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a Cascada in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
