Brown Buick Lucernes at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Buick Lucerne in our paint database was in 2006, with 2 brown paint shades named Antique Bronze and Cashmere.

The last offering of Brown on a Buick Lucerne in our paint database was in 2011, with 2 shades of brown paint named Light Bronze and Sand Beige.

In our database we found 11 offerings of Brown in 6 of the 6 production years of the Lucerne (2006-2011). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on a Lucerne in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
