Gray Buick Encores at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Buick Encore in our paint database was in 2013, with 3 gray paint shades named Carbon Black, Quicksilver and Satin Steel Gray.

The latest offering of Gray on a Buick Encore is available now in the 2026 model year, with 2 shades of gray paint named Ceramic and Moonstone Gray.

In our database we found 31 offerings of Gray in 14 of the 14 production years of the Encore (2013-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
