Brown Buick Regals at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Buick Regal in our paint database was in 2000, with 3 brown paint shades named Auburn Nightmist, Gold and Light Sandrift.

The last offering of Brown on a Buick Regal in our paint database was in 2012, with one shade of brown paint named Gold Mist.

In our database we found 14 offerings of Brown in 7 of the 7 production years of the Regal (2000-2004 and 2011-2012). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on a Regal in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
