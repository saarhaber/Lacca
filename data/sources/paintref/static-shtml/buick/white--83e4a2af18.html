White Buick Enclaves at PaintRef.com


The first White Buick Enclave in our paint database was in 2008, with 2 white paint shades named White and White Diamond.

The latest offering of White on a Buick Enclave is available now in the 2026 model year, with 2 shades of white paint named Iridescent White and Summit White.

In our database we found 38 offerings of White in 19 of the 19 production years of the Enclave (2008-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
