Blue Buick Regals at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Buick Regal in our paint database was in 2000, with 2 blue paint shades named Navy Blue and Regal Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Buick Regal in our paint database was in 2020, with one shade of blue paint named Dark Moon Blue.

In our database we found 17 offerings of Blue in 14 of the 14 production years of the Regal (2000-2003 and 2011-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
