Gray Buick Veranos at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Buick Verano in our paint database was in 2012, with 2 gray paint shades named Cyber Gray and Switchblade Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a Buick Verano in our paint database was in 2017, with 2 shades of gray paint named Graphite Gray and Quicksilver.

In our database we found 16 offerings of Gray in 6 of the 6 production years of the Verano (2012-2017). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
