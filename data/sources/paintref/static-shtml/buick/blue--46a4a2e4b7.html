Blue Buick Centurys at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Buick Century in our paint database was in 2000, with 2 blue paint shades named Navy Blue and Regal Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Buick Century in our paint database was in 2005, with one shade of blue paint named Blue Ice.

In our database we found 6 offerings of Blue in 5 of the 5 production years of the Century (2000-2003 and 2005). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a Century in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
