Gray Buick Lucernes at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Buick Lucerne in our paint database was in 2006, with 2 gray paint shades named Gunmetal and Light Tarnished Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a Buick Lucerne in our paint database was in 2011, with 2 shades of gray paint named Cyber Gray and Quicksilver.

In our database we found 18 offerings of Gray in 6 of the 6 production years of the Lucerne (2006-2011). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
