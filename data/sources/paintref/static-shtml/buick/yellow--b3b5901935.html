Yellow Buick Encores at PaintRef.com


The first Yellow Buick Encore in our paint database was in 2022, with one yellow paint shade named Sunset Glow.

The last offering of Yellow on a Buick Encore in our paint database was in 2023, with one shade of yellow paint named Sunset Glow.

In our database we found 2 offerings of Yellow in 2 of the 2 production years of the Encore (2022-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
