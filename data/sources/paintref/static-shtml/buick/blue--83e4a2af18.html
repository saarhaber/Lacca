Blue Buick Enclaves at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Buick Enclave in our paint database was in 2008, with 2 blue paint shades named Dark Ming Blue and Transition Blue.

The latest offering of Blue on a Buick Enclave is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of blue paint named Ocean Blue.

In our database we found 23 offerings of Blue in 16 of the 16 production years of the Enclave (2008-2017 and 2020 and 2022-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on an Enclave in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
