Gray Buick Regals at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Buick Regal in our paint database was in 2000, with one gray paint shade named Galaxy Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a Buick Regal in our paint database was in 2020, with 2 shades of gray paint named Quicksilver and Satin Steel.

In our database we found 38 offerings of Gray in 15 of the 15 production years of the Regal (2000-2004 and 2011-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
