Red Buick LaCrosses at PaintRef.com


The first Red Buick LaCrosse in our paint database was in 2005, with one red paint shade named Sport Red.

The last offering of Red on a Buick LaCrosse in our paint database was in 2019, with 2 shades of red paint named Red Quartz and Rioja Red.

In our database we found 22 offerings of Red in 15 of the 15 production years of the LaCrosse (2005-2019). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a LaCrosse in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
