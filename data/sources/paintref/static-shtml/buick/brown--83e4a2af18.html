Brown Buick Enclaves at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Buick Enclave in our paint database was in 2008, with 2 brown paint shades named Cocoa and Gold Mist.

The last offering of Brown on a Buick Enclave in our paint database was in 2019, with one shade of brown paint named Havana.

In our database we found 16 offerings of Brown in 11 of the 11 production years of the Enclave (2008-2013 and 2015-2019). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
