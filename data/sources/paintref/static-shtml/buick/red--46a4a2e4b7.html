Red Buick Centurys at PaintRef.com


The first Red Buick Century in our paint database was in 2000, with 2 red paint shades named Dark Toreador and Light Toreador.

The last offering of Red on a Buick Century in our paint database was in 2005, with one shade of red paint named Sport Red.

In our database we found 7 offerings of Red in 6 of the 6 production years of the Century (2000-2005). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a Century in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
