Blue Buick LaCrosses at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Buick LaCrosse in our paint database was in 2005, with 2 blue paint shades named Blue Ice and Superior Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Buick LaCrosse in our paint database was in 2019, with 2 shades of blue paint named Carrageen and Dark Moon Blue.

In our database we found 21 offerings of Blue in 14 of the 14 production years of the LaCrosse (2005-2017 and 2019). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a LaCrosse in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
