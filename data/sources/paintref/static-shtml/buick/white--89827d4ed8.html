White Buick Rendezvouss at PaintRef.com


The first White Buick Rendezvous in our paint database was in 2002, with 2 white paint shades named Bright White and White.

The last offering of White on a Buick Rendezvous in our paint database was in 2007, with one shade of white paint named Olympic White.

In our database we found 7 offerings of White in 6 of the 6 production years of the Rendezvous (2002-2007). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of White on a Rendezvous in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
