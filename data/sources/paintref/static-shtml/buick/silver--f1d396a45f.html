Silver Buick Allures at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Buick Allure in our paint database was in 2007, with 2 silver paint shades named Dark Tarnished Silver and Light Tarnished Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a Buick Allure in our paint database was in 2009, with 2 shades of silver paint named Dark Tarnished Silver and Switchblade Silver.

In our database we found 6 offerings of Silver in 3 of the 3 production years of the Allure (2007-2009). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Silver on an Allure in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
