Gray Buick Enclaves at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Buick Enclave in our paint database was in 2008, with 4 gray paint shades named Carbon Flash, Dark Brahma Jewel, Light Tarnished Silver and Platinum Ice.

The latest offering of Gray on a Buick Enclave is available now in the 2026 model year, with 2 shades of gray paint named Moonstone Gray and Stone.

In our database we found 61 offerings of Gray in 19 of the 19 production years of the Enclave (2008-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on an Enclave in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
