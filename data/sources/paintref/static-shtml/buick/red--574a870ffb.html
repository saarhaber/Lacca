Red Buick Regals at PaintRef.com


The first Red Buick Regal in our paint database was in 2000, with 3 red paint shades named Dark Toreador, Light Toreador and Red.

The last offering of Red on a Buick Regal in our paint database was in 2020, with 2 shades of red paint named Rioja Red and Sport Red.

In our database we found 24 offerings of Red in 15 of the 15 production years of the Regal (2000-2004 and 2011-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a Regal in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
