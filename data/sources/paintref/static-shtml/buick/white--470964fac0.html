White Buick Lucernes at PaintRef.com


The first White Buick Lucerne in our paint database was in 2006, with 2 white paint shades named White and White Gold Pearl.

The last offering of White on a Buick Lucerne in our paint database was in 2011, with 2 shades of white paint named White Diamond and White Opal.

In our database we found 14 offerings of White in 6 of the 6 production years of the Lucerne (2006-2011). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of White on a Lucerne in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
