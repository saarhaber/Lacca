Silver Buick Regals at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Buick Regal in our paint database was in 2000, with one silver paint shade named Galaxy Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a Buick Regal in our paint database was in 2020, with one shade of silver paint named Quicksilver.

In our database we found 18 offerings of Silver in 15 of the 15 production years of the Regal (2000-2004 and 2011-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
