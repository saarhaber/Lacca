Brown Buick LaCrosses at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Buick LaCrosse in our paint database was in 2005, with one brown paint shade named Cashmere.

The last offering of Brown on a Buick LaCrosse in our paint database was in 2016, with one shade of brown paint named Dark Chocolate.

In our database we found 17 offerings of Brown in 12 of the 12 production years of the LaCrosse (2005-2016). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on a LaCrosse in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
