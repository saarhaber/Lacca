White Buick Encores at PaintRef.com


The first White Buick Encore in our paint database was in 2013, with one white paint shade named Snowflake White.

The latest offering of White on a Buick Encore is available now in the 2026 model year, with 2 shades of white paint named Summit White and White Frost.

In our database we found 25 offerings of White in 14 of the 14 production years of the Encore (2013-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
