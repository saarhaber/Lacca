Black Buick Enclaves at PaintRef.com


The first Black Buick Enclave in our paint database was in 2008, with one black paint shade named Carbon Flash.

The latest offering of Black on a Buick Enclave is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of black paint named Ebony Twilight.

In our database we found 20 offerings of Black in 19 of the 19 production years of the Enclave (2008-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
