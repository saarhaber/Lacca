Red Honda Ridgelines at PaintRef.com


The first Red Honda Ridgeline in our paint database was in 2006, with one red paint shade named Red Rock.

The last offering of Red on a Honda Ridgeline in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of red paint named Radiant Red.

In our database we found 19 offerings of Red in 18 of the 18 production years of the Ridgeline (2006-2014 and 2017-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
