Blue Honda Accords at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Honda Accord in our paint database was in 1994, with 9 blue paint shades named Azure Blue, Dark Green, Fresco Blue, Grayish Blue Green, Isle Green, Malachite Green, Pacific Blue, Sage Green and Sherwood Green.

The last offering of Blue on a Honda Accord in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of blue paint named Canyon River Blue and Still Night.

In our database we found 131 offerings of Blue in 30 of the 30 production years of the Accord (1994-1997 and 2000-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on an Accord in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
