Blue Honda Preludes at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Honda Prelude in our paint database was in 1994, with 7 blue paint shades named Azure Blue, Fresco Blue, Isle Green, Malachite Green, Pacific Blue, Sage Green and Sherwood Green.

The last offering of Blue on a Honda Prelude in our paint database was in 2001, with 2 shades of blue paint named Electron Blue and Nighthawk Black.

In our database we found 22 offerings of Blue in 7 of the 7 production years of the Prelude (1994-1995 and 1997-2001). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a Prelude in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
