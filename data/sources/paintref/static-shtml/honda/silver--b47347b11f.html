Silver Honda Ridgelines at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Honda Ridgeline in our paint database was in 2006, with one silver paint shade named Billet Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a Honda Ridgeline in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of silver paint named Solar Silver.

In our database we found 18 offerings of Silver in 18 of the 18 production years of the Ridgeline (2006-2014 and 2017-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
