Gray Honda Accords at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Honda Accord in our paint database was in 1994, with 4 gray paint shades named Dark Charcoal, Night Shade Gray, Phantom Gray and Sebring Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a Honda Accord in our paint database was in 2025, with 3 shades of gray paint named Meteorite Gray, Solar Silver and Urban Gray.

In our database we found 131 offerings of Gray in 31 of the 31 production years of the Accord (1994-1995 and 1997-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on an Accord in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
