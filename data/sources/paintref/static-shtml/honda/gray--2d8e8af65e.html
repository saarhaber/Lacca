Gray Honda Preludes at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Honda Prelude in our paint database was in 1994, with 3 gray paint shades named Night Shade Gray, Phantom Gray and Sebring Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a Honda Prelude in our paint database was in 2002, with one shade of gray paint named Satin Silver.

In our database we found 9 offerings of Gray in 7 of the 7 production years of the Prelude (1994-1997 and 2000-2002). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on a Prelude in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
