White Honda Odysseys at PaintRef.com


The first White Honda Odyssey in our paint database was in 1996, with one white paint shade named Frost White.

The last offering of White on a Honda Odyssey in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of white paint named Platinum White.

In our database we found 33 offerings of White in 28 of the 28 production years of the Odyssey (1996-1998 and 2000-2001 and 2003-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of White on an Odyssey in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
