White Honda Pilots at PaintRef.com


The first White Honda Pilot in our paint database was in 2003, with one white paint shade named Taffeta White.

The last offering of White on a Honda Pilot in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of white paint named Platinum White.

In our database we found 33 offerings of White in 23 of the 23 production years of the Pilot (2003-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of White on a Pilot in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
