Green Honda Elements at PaintRef.com


The first Green Honda Element in our paint database was in 2003, with 2 green paint shades named Clover Green and Galapagos Green.

The last offering of Green on a Honda Element in our paint database was in 2008, with one shade of green paint named Kiwi Green.

In our database we found 6 offerings of Green in 5 of the 5 production years of the Element (2003-2005 and 2007-2008). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Green on an Element in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
