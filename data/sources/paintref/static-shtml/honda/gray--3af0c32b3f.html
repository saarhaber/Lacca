Gray Plymouth Furys at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Plymouth Fury in our paint database was in 1969, with one gray paint shade named Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a Plymouth Fury in our paint database was in 1978, with one shade of gray paint named Pewter Gray.

In our database we found 9 offerings of Gray in 8 of the 8 production years of the Fury (1969-1973 and 1975-1976 and 1978). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
