Silver Honda Pilots at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Honda Pilot in our paint database was in 2003, with one silver paint shade named Starlight Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a Honda Pilot in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of silver paint named Lunar Silver.

In our database we found 27 offerings of Silver in 23 of the 23 production years of the Pilot (2003-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Silver on a Pilot in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
