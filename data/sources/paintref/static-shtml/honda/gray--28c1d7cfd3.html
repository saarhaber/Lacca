Gray Honda Odysseys at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Honda Odyssey in our paint database was in 1999, with one gray paint shade named New Quartz Gray.

The last offering of Gray on a Honda Odyssey in our paint database was in 2025, with 3 shades of gray paint named Modern Steel, Solar Silver and Sonic Gray.

In our database we found 88 offerings of Gray in 27 of the 27 production years of the Odyssey (1999-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on an Odyssey in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
