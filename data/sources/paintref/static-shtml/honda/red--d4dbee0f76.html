Red Honda Accords at PaintRef.com


The first Red Honda Accord in our paint database was in 1994, with 3 red paint shades named Bordeaux Red, Milano Red and Torino Red.

The last offering of Red on a Honda Accord in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of red paint named Radiant Red.

In our database we found 88 offerings of Red in 32 of the 32 production years of the Accord (1994-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
