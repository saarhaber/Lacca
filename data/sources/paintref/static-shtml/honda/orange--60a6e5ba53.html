Orange Honda Civics at PaintRef.com


The first Orange Honda Civic in our paint database was in 1996, with 2 orange paint shades named Coral Orange and Island Coral.

The last offering of Orange on a Honda Civic in our paint database was in 2023, with one shade of orange paint named Blazing Orange.

In our database we found 25 offerings of Orange in 17 of the 17 production years of the Civic (1996-1999 and 2009-2016 and 2019-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Orange on a Civic in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
