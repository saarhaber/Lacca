Orange Honda Fits at PaintRef.com


The first Orange Honda Fit in our paint database was in 2007, with one orange paint shade named Blaze Orange.

The last offering of Orange on a Honda Fit in our paint database was in 2020, with one shade of orange paint named Orange Fury.

In our database we found 11 offerings of Orange in 11 of the 11 production years of the Fit (2007-2014 and 2018-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Orange on a Fit in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
