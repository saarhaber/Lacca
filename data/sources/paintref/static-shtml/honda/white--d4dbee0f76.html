White Honda Accords at PaintRef.com


The first White Honda Accord in our paint database was in 1994, with one white paint shade named Frost White.

The last offering of White on a Honda Accord in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of white paint named Platinum White.

In our database we found 75 offerings of White in 32 of the 32 production years of the Accord (1994-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of White on an Accord in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
