Red Plymouth Furys at PaintRef.com


The first Red Plymouth Fury in our paint database was in 1965, with 2 red paint shades named Medium Red and Ruby.

The last offering of Red on a Plymouth Fury in our paint database was in 1978, with 2 shades of red paint named Brite Canyon Red and Tapestry Red Sunfire.

In our database we found 19 offerings of Red in 9 of the 9 production years of the Fury (1965 and 1969-1973 and 1975-1976 and 1978). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
