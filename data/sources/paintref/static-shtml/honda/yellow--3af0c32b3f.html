Yellow Plymouth Furys at PaintRef.com


The first Yellow Plymouth Fury in our paint database was in 1969, with 3 yellow paint shades named Bahama Yellow, Sunfire Yellow and Yellow Gold.

The last offering of Yellow on a Plymouth Fury in our paint database was in 1978, with one shade of yellow paint named Jasmine Yellow.

In our database we found 10 offerings of Yellow in 6 of the 6 production years of the Fury (1969-1970 and 1972-1973 and 1975 and 1978). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
