Brown Honda Odysseys at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Honda Odyssey in our paint database was in 1999, with one brown paint shade named Beige.

The last offering of Brown on a Honda Odyssey in our paint database was in 2013, with one shade of brown paint named Mocha.

In our database we found 16 offerings of Brown in 14 of the 14 production years of the Odyssey (1999-2005 and 2007-2013). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on an Odyssey in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
