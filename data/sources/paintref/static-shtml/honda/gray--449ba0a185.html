Gray Honda S2000s at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Honda S2000 in our paint database was in 2000, with one gray paint shade named Silver Stone.

The last offering of Gray on a Honda S2000 in our paint database was in 2009, with one shade of gray paint named Chicane Silver.

In our database we found 17 offerings of Gray in 10 of the 10 production years of the S2000 (2000-2009). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
