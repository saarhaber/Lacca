Green Honda Odysseys at PaintRef.com


The first Green Honda Odyssey in our paint database was in 1996, with one green paint shade named Sage Green.

The last offering of Green on a Honda Odyssey in our paint database was in 2021, with one shade of green paint named Forest Mist.

In our database we found 21 offerings of Green in 18 of the 18 production years of the Odyssey (1996-2003 and 2005-2010 and 2018-2021). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
