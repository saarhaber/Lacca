Silver Honda Civics at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Honda Civic in our paint database was in 1994, with 2 silver paint shades named Cashmere Silver and Sebring Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a Honda Civic in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of silver paint named Solar Silver.

In our database we found 107 offerings of Silver in 31 of the 31 production years of the Civic (1994 and 1996-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Silver on a Civic in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
