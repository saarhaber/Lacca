Brown Honda Civics at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Honda Civic in our paint database was in 1994, with one brown paint shade named Cashmere Silver.

The last offering of Brown on a Honda Civic in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of brown paint named Sand Dune.

In our database we found 11 offerings of Brown in 9 of the 9 production years of the Civic (1994 and 2001-2002 and 2007-2008 and 2013-2015 and 2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on a Civic in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
