Blue Honda Elements at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Honda Element in our paint database was in 2003, with one blue paint shade named Eternal Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Honda Element in our paint database was in 2011, with one shade of blue paint named Royal Blue.

In our database we found 16 offerings of Blue in 9 of the 9 production years of the Element (2003-2011). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
