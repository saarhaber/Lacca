Red Honda Odysseys at PaintRef.com


The first Red Honda Odyssey in our paint database was in 1996, with one red paint shade named Bordeaux Red.

The last offering of Red on a Honda Odyssey in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of red paint named Radiant Red.

In our database we found 33 offerings of Red in 28 of the 28 production years of the Odyssey (1996-1999 and 2002-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
