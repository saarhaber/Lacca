White Honda Ridgelines at PaintRef.com


The first White Honda Ridgeline in our paint database was in 2006, with one white paint shade named Taffeta White.

The last offering of White on a Honda Ridgeline in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of white paint named Platinum White.

In our database we found 18 offerings of White in 18 of the 18 production years of the Ridgeline (2006-2014 and 2017-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of White on a Ridgeline in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
