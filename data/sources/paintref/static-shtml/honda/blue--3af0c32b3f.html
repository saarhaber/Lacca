Blue Plymouth Furys at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Plymouth Fury in our paint database was in 1965, with 6 blue paint shades named Dark Blue, Dark Turquoise, Light Blue, Light Turquoise, Medium Blue and Turquoise.

The last offering of Blue on a Plymouth Fury in our paint database was in 1978, with 3 shades of blue paint named Cadet Blue, Starlight Blue Sunfire and Wedgewood Blue.

In our database we found 34 offerings of Blue in 9 of the 9 production years of the Fury (1965 and 1969-1973 and 1975-1976 and 1978). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
