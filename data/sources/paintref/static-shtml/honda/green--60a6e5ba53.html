Green Honda Civics at PaintRef.com


The first Green Honda Civic in our paint database was in 1994, with 7 green paint shades named Aztec Green, Aztec Green Pearl, Fiesta Green, Isle Green, Malachite Green, Sage Green and Sherwood Green.

The last offering of Green on a Honda Civic in our paint database was in 2019, with one shade of green paint named Tonic Yellow.

In our database we found 53 offerings of Green in 21 of the 21 production years of the Civic (1994-2005 and 2009 and 2012-2019). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Green on a Civic in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
