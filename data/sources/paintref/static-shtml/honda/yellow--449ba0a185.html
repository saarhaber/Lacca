Yellow Honda S2000s at PaintRef.com


The first Yellow Honda S2000 in our paint database was in 2001, with one yellow paint shade named Spa Yellow.

The last offering of Yellow on a Honda S2000 in our paint database was in 2009, with one shade of yellow paint named Rio Yellow.

In our database we found 9 offerings of Yellow in 9 of the 9 production years of the S2000 (2001-2009). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
