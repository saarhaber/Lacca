Green Plymouth Furys at PaintRef.com


The first Green Plymouth Fury in our paint database was in 1965, with 3 green paint shades named Dark Turquoise, Light Turquoise and Turquoise.

The last offering of Green on a Plymouth Fury in our paint database was in 1978, with 2 shades of green paint named Augusta Green Sunfire and Mint Green.

In our database we found 28 offerings of Green in 9 of the 9 production years of the Fury (1965 and 1969-1973 and 1975-1976 and 1978). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
