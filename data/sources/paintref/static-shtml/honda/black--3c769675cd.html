Black Honda Pilots at PaintRef.com


The first Black Honda Pilot in our paint database was in 2007, with one black paint shade named Formal Black.

The last offering of Black on a Honda Pilot in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of black paint named Crystal Black.

In our database we found 19 offerings of Black in 19 of the 19 production years of the Pilot (2007-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
