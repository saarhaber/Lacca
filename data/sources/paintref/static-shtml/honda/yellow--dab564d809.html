Yellow Honda Fits at PaintRef.com


The first Yellow Honda Fit in our paint database was in 2012, with one yellow paint shade named Hellos Yellow.

The last offering of Yellow on a Honda Fit in our paint database was in 2019, with one shade of yellow paint named Helios Yellow.

In our database we found 8 offerings of Yellow in 7 of the 7 production years of the Fit (2012 and 2014-2019). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
