Red Honda Insights at PaintRef.com


The first Red Honda Insight in our paint database was in 2000, with one red paint shade named Formula Red.

The last offering of Red on a Honda Insight in our paint database was in 2022, with one shade of red paint named Radiant Red.

In our database we found 17 offerings of Red in 16 of the 16 production years of the Insight (2000-2006 and 2010-2014 and 2019-2022). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
