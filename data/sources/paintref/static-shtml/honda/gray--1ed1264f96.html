Gray Honda Insights at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Honda Insight in our paint database was in 2000, with one gray paint shade named Silver Stone.

The last offering of Gray on a Honda Insight in our paint database was in 2022, with 2 shades of gray paint named Lunar Silver and Modern Steel.

In our database we found 31 offerings of Gray in 16 of the 16 production years of the Insight (2000-2006 and 2010-2014 and 2019-2022). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
