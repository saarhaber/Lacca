Green Honda Accords at PaintRef.com


The first Green Honda Accord in our paint database was in 1994, with 6 green paint shades named Dark Green, Grayish Blue Green, Isle Green, Malachite Green, Sage Green and Sherwood Green.

The last offering of Green on a Honda Accord in our paint database was in 2011, with one shade of green paint named Opal Sage.

In our database we found 49 offerings of Green in 16 of the 16 production years of the Accord (1994-2005 and 2008-2011). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Green on an Accord in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
