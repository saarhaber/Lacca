Purple Honda Accords at PaintRef.com


The first Purple Honda Accord in our paint database was in 1995, with one purple paint shade named Heather Mist.

The last offering of Purple on a Honda Accord in our paint database was in 1999, with 2 shades of purple paint named Heather Mist and Raisin.

In our database we found 9 offerings of Purple in 5 of the 5 production years of the Accord (1995-1999). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
