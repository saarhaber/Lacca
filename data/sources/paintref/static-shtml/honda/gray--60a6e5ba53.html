Gray Honda Civics at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Honda Civic in our paint database was in 1994, with 5 gray paint shades named Dark Charcoal, Horizon Gray, Night Shade Gray, Phantom Gray and Sebring Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a Honda Civic in our paint database was in 2025, with 4 shades of gray paint named Meteorite Gray, Solar Silver, Sonic Gray and Urban Gray.

In our database we found 195 offerings of Gray in 32 of the 32 production years of the Civic (1994-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on a Civic in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
