Blue Honda Passports at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Honda Passport in our paint database was in 1995, with 2 blue paint shades named Indigo Blue and Viridian Green.

The latest offering of Blue on a Honda Passport is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of blue paint named Obsidian Blue.

In our database we found 14 offerings of Blue in 12 of the 12 production years of the Passport (1995 and 2000-2002 and 2019-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a Passport in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
