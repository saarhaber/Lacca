Black Plymouth Furys at PaintRef.com


The first Black Plymouth Fury in our paint database was in 1965, with one black paint shade named Ebony.

The last offering of Black on a Plymouth Fury in our paint database was in 1978, with one shade of black paint named Formal Black.

In our database we found 9 offerings of Black in 9 of the 9 production years of the Fury (1965 and 1969-1973 and 1975-1976 and 1978). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
