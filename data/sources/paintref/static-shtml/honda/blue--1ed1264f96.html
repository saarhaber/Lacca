Blue Honda Insights at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Honda Insight in our paint database was in 2001, with 2 blue paint shades named Aquamarine Blue and Montecarlo Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Honda Insight in our paint database was in 2022, with one shade of blue paint named Cosmic Blue.

In our database we found 20 offerings of Blue in 15 of the 15 production years of the Insight (2001-2006 and 2010-2014 and 2019-2022). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
