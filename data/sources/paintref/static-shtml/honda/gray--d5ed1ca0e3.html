Gray Honda Passports at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Honda Passport in our paint database was in 1995, with 2 gray paint shades named Iron Gray and Light Silver.

The latest offering of Gray on a Honda Passport is available now in the 2026 model year, with 2 shades of gray paint named Modern Steel and Sonic Gray.

In our database we found 29 offerings of Gray in 16 of the 16 production years of the Passport (1995-2002 and 2019-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
