Brown Plymouth Furys at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Plymouth Fury in our paint database was in 1965, with 4 brown paint shades named Copper, Gold, Light Tan and Medium Tan.

The last offering of Brown on a Plymouth Fury in our paint database was in 1978, with 2 shades of brown paint named Golden Fawn and Spanish Gold.

In our database we found 45 offerings of Brown in 9 of the 9 production years of the Fury (1965 and 1969-1973 and 1975-1976 and 1978). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on a Fury in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
