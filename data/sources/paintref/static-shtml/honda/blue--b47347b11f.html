Blue Honda Ridgelines at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Honda Ridgeline in our paint database was in 2006, with 2 blue paint shades named Nighthawk Black and Steel Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Honda Ridgeline in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of blue paint named Diffused Sky Blue.

In our database we found 20 offerings of Blue in 18 of the 18 production years of the Ridgeline (2006-2014 and 2017-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a Ridgeline in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
