The first Silver Honda Prologue in our paint database was in 2024, with one silver paint shade named Mercury Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a Honda Prologue in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of silver paint named Mercury Silver.

We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
