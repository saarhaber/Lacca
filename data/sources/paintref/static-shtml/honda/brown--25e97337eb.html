Brown Ford Interceptors at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Ford Interceptor in our paint database was in 2020, with 3 brown paint shades named Arizona Beige, Kodiak Brown and Medium Brown.

The last offering of Brown on a Ford Interceptor in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of brown paint named Arizona Beige and Kodiak Brown.

In our database we found 16 offerings of Brown in 6 of the 6 production years of the Interceptor (2020-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on an Interceptor in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
