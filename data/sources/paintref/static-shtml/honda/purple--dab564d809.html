Purple Honda Fits at PaintRef.com


The first Purple Honda Fit in our paint database was in 2008, with one purple paint shade named Blackberry Pearl.

The last offering of Purple on a Honda Fit in our paint database was in 2017, with one shade of purple paint named Passion Berry.

In our database we found 10 offerings of Purple in 8 of the 8 production years of the Fit (2008-2010 and 2013-2017). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
