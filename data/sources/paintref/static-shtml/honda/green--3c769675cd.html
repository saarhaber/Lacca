Green Honda Pilots at PaintRef.com


The first Green Honda Pilot in our paint database was in 2003, with one green paint shade named Evergreen.

The last offering of Green on a Honda Pilot in our paint database was in 2023, with one shade of green paint named Black Forest.

In our database we found 11 offerings of Green in 11 of the 11 production years of the Pilot (2003 and 2006-2008 and 2016-2021 and 2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
