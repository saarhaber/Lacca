Blue Ford Interceptors at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Ford Interceptor in our paint database was in 2020, with 5 blue paint shades named Blue, Dark Blue, Light Blue, Norsea Blue and Royal Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Ford Interceptor in our paint database was in 2025, with 4 shades of blue paint named Dark Blue, Light Blue, Police Green and Royal Blue.

In our database we found 24 offerings of Blue in 6 of the 6 production years of the Interceptor (2020-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
