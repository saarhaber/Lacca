Brown Honda Accords at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Honda Accord in our paint database was in 1994, with one brown paint shade named Cashmere Silver.

The last offering of Brown on a Honda Accord in our paint database was in 2020, with one shade of brown paint named Champagne Frost.

In our database we found 51 offerings of Brown in 21 of the 21 production years of the Accord (1994-1995 and 2000-2010 and 2013-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on an Accord in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
