Gray Ford Interceptors at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Ford Interceptor in our paint database was in 2020, with 5 gray paint shades named Iconic Silver, Medium Titanium, Silver Gray, Smokestone and Sterling Gray.

The last offering of Gray on a Ford Interceptor in our paint database was in 2025, with 4 shades of gray paint named Carbonized Gray, Iconic Silver, Silver Gray and Sterling Gray.

In our database we found 30 offerings of Gray in 6 of the 6 production years of the Interceptor (2020-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
