Blue Honda Civics at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Honda Civic in our paint database was in 1994, with 11 blue paint shades named Aztec Green, Aztec Green Pearl, Azure Blue, Captiva Blue, Harvard Blue, Harvard Blue Pearl, Isle Green, Malachite Green, Pacific Blue, Sage Green and Sherwood Green.

The last offering of Blue on a Honda Civic in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of blue paint named Blue Lagoon and Boost Blue.

In our database we found 175 offerings of Blue in 32 of the 32 production years of the Civic (1994-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a Civic in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
