Red Honda Civics at PaintRef.com


The first Red Honda Civic in our paint database was in 1994, with 5 red paint shades named Bordeaux Red, Camellia Red, Camellia Red Pearl, Milano Red and Torino Red.

The last offering of Red on a Honda Civic in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of red paint named Rallye Red.

In our database we found 122 offerings of Red in 32 of the 32 production years of the Civic (1994-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a Civic in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
