Blue Honda Odysseys at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Honda Odyssey in our paint database was in 1996, with 3 blue paint shades named Azure Blue, Nocturne Blue and Sage Green.

The last offering of Blue on a Honda Odyssey in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of blue paint named Smoke Blue.

In our database we found 74 offerings of Blue in 30 of the 30 production years of the Odyssey (1996-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
