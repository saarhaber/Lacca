Yellow Chevrolet Novas at PaintRef.com


The first Yellow Chevrolet Nova in our paint database was in 1962, with one yellow paint shade named Coronna Cream.

The last offering of Yellow on a Chevrolet Nova in our paint database was in 1979, with one shade of yellow paint named Bright Yellow.

In our database we found 26 offerings of Yellow in 17 of the 17 production years of the Nova (1962 and 1964-1979). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
