Orange Nissan Versas at PaintRef.com


The first Orange Nissan Versa in our paint database was in 2007, with one orange paint shade named Scarlet Red.

The last offering of Orange on a Nissan Versa in our paint database was in 2022, with one shade of orange paint named Monarch Orange.

In our database we found 8 offerings of Orange in 8 of the 8 production years of the Versa (2007-2009 and 2011 and 2017 and 2020-2022). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
