Blue Nissan 240SXs at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Nissan 240SX in our paint database was in 1989, with 2 blue paint shades named Midnight Blue and Platinum Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Nissan 240SX in our paint database was in 1998, with one shade of blue paint named Starfire Blue.

In our database we found 15 offerings of Blue in 10 of the 10 production years of the 240SX (1989-1998). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
