Gray Nissan GT-Rs at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Nissan GT-R in our paint database was in 2009, with 3 gray paint shades named Airstream, Desert Shadow and Gun.

The last offering of Gray on a Nissan GT-R in our paint database was in 2025, with 3 shades of gray paint named Gun Metal, Stealth Gray and Super Silver.

In our database we found 38 offerings of Gray in 17 of the 17 production years of the GT-R (2009-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
