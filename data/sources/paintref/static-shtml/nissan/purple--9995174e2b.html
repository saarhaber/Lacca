Purple Nissan Maximas at PaintRef.com


The first Purple Nissan Maxima in our paint database was in 1988, with one purple paint shade named Medium Lavender.

The last offering of Purple on a Nissan Maxima in our paint database was in 1996, with one shade of purple paint named Silver Amethyst.

In our database we found 3 offerings of Purple in 3 of the 3 production years of the Maxima (1988 and 1995-1996). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
