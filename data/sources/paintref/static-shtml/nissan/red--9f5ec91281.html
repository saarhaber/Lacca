Red NXs at PaintRef.com


The first Red NX in our paint database was in 1991, with 2 red paint shades named Aztec Red and Cherry Red.

The last offering of Red on an NX in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of red paint named Redline.

In our database we found 17 offerings of Red in 14 of the 14 production years of the NX (1991-1993 and 2015-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
