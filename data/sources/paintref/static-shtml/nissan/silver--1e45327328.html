Silver Nissan 350Zs at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Nissan 350Z in our paint database was in 2003, with 2 silver paint shades named Brilliant Silver and Polished Titanium Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a Nissan 350Z in our paint database was in 2009, with one shade of silver paint named Carbon Silver.

In our database we found 13 offerings of Silver in 7 of the 7 production years of the 350Z (2003-2009). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
