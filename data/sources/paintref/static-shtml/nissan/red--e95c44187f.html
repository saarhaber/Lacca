The first Red Nissan Murano in our paint database was in 2005, with one red paint shade named Merlot.

The last offering of Red on a Nissan Murano in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of red paint named Scarlet Ember.

In our database we found 25 offerings of Red in 21 of the 21 production years of the Murano (2005-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
