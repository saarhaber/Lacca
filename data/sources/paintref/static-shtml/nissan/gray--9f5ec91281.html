Gray NXs at PaintRef.com


The first Gray NX in our paint database was in 1991, with 2 gray paint shades named Satin Silver and Slate Gray.

The last offering of Gray on an NX in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of gray paint named Atomic Silver and Cloudburst Gray.

In our database we found 29 offerings of Gray in 13 of the 13 production years of the NX (1991 and 1993 and 2015-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
