White Nissan Sentras at PaintRef.com


The first White Nissan Sentra in our paint database was in 1982, with one white paint shade named Alpine White.

The last offering of White on a Nissan Sentra in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of white paint named Aspen White and Fresh Powder.

In our database we found 62 offerings of White in 44 of the 44 production years of the Sentra (1982-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
