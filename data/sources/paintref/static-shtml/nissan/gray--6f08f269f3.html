Gray Nissan 200SXs at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Nissan 200SX in our paint database was in 1980, with 2 gray paint shades named Diamond Mist and Medium Gray.

The last offering of Gray on a Nissan 200SX in our paint database was in 1988, with one shade of gray paint named Platinum.

In our database we found 15 offerings of Gray in 8 of the 8 production years of the 200SX (1980-1986 and 1988). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
