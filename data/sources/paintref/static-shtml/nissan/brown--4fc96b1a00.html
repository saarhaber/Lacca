Brown Nissan Sentras at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Nissan Sentra in our paint database was in 1982, with 2 brown paint shades named Dark Brown and French Beige.

The last offering of Brown on a Nissan Sentra in our paint database was in 2012, with 2 shades of brown paint named Choco Glass and Toasted Almond.

In our database we found 44 offerings of Brown in 31 of the 31 production years of the Sentra (1982-2012). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
