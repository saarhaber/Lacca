Red Nissan Frontiers at PaintRef.com


The first Red Nissan Frontier in our paint database was in 2000, with 2 red paint shades named Aztec Red and Salsa Red.

The last offering of Red on a Nissan Frontier in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of red paint named Cardinal Red and Red Alert.

In our database we found 49 offerings of Red in 26 of the 26 production years of the Frontier (2000-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a Frontier in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
