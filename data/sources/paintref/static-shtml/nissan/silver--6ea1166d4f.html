Silver Nissan Pathfinders at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Nissan Pathfinder in our paint database was in 1987, with one silver paint shade named Platinum.

The last offering of Silver on a Nissan Pathfinder in our paint database was in 2011, with one shade of silver paint named Radiant Silver.

In our database we found 18 offerings of Silver in 17 of the 17 production years of the Pathfinder (1987-1989 and 1997-2000 and 2002-2011). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
