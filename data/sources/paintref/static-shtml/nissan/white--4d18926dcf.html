White Stanzas at PaintRef.com


The first White Stanza in our paint database was in 1982, with 2 white paint shades named Alpine White and White.

The last offering of White on a Stanza in our paint database was in 1992, with 2 shades of white paint named Satin White and Super White.

In our database we found 15 offerings of White in 11 of the 11 production years of the Stanza (1982-1992). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
