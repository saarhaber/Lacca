Gray Nissan Leafs at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Nissan Leaf in our paint database was in 2011, with one gray paint shade named Airstream.

The last offering of Gray on a Nissan Leaf in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of gray paint named Brilliant Silver and Gun.

In our database we found 27 offerings of Gray in 15 of the 15 production years of the Leaf (2011-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
