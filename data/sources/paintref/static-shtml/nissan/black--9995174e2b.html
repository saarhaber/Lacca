Black Nissan Maximas at PaintRef.com


The first Black Nissan Maxima in our paint database was in 1982, with one black paint shade named Thunder Black.

The last offering of Black on a Nissan Maxima in our paint database was in 2023, with one shade of black paint named Super Black.

In our database we found 47 offerings of Black in 39 of the 39 production years of the Maxima (1982-1983 and 1985-1995 and 1997-2005 and 2007-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
