Red Nissan Leafs at PaintRef.com


The first Red Nissan Leaf in our paint database was in 2011, with one red paint shade named Force Red.

The last offering of Red on a Nissan Leaf in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of red paint named Scarlet Ember.

In our database we found 16 offerings of Red in 14 of the 14 production years of the Leaf (2011-2020 and 2022-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
