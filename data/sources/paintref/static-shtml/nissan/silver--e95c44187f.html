Silver Nissan Muranos at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Nissan Murano in our paint database was in 2003, with one silver paint shade named Brilliant Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a Nissan Murano in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of silver paint named Champagn Silver.

In our database we found 9 offerings of Silver in 8 of the 8 production years of the Murano (2003-2005 and 2008-2010 and 2012 and 2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
