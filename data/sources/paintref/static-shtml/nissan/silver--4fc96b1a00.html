Silver Nissan Sentras at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Nissan Sentra in our paint database was in 1982, with one silver paint shade named Diamond Mist.

The last offering of Silver on a Nissan Sentra in our paint database was in 2008, with one shade of silver paint named Radiant Silver.

In our database we found 27 offerings of Silver in 21 of the 21 production years of the Sentra (1982-1983 and 1986-1990 and 1992-1997 and 2000-2006 and 2008). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
