Blue Armadas at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Armada in our paint database was in 1996, with one blue paint shade named Blauw.

The last offering of Blue on an Armada in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of blue paint named Deep Ocean Blue.

In our database we found 27 offerings of Blue in 22 of the 22 production years of the Armada (1996 and 2005-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
