Red Nissan 200SXs at PaintRef.com


The first Red Nissan 200SX in our paint database was in 1980, with 3 red paint shades named Cabernet, Maroon Mist and Rallye Red.

The last offering of Red on a Nissan 200SX in our paint database was in 1998, with 2 shades of red paint named Aztec Red and Sunset Red.

In our database we found 29 offerings of Red in 12 of the 12 production years of the 200SX (1980-1988 and 1996-1998). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
