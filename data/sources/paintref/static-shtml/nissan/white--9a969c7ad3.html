White Nissan NVs at PaintRef.com


The first White Nissan NV in our paint database was in 2011, with one white paint shade named Blizzard White.

The last offering of White on a Nissan NV in our paint database was in 2021, with one shade of white paint named Glacier White.

In our database we found 21 offerings of White in 11 of the 11 production years of the NV (2011-2021). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
