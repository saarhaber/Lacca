Red Nissan Titans at PaintRef.com


The first Red Nissan Titan in our paint database was in 2004, with one red paint shade named Sparkle Red.

The last offering of Red on a Nissan Titan in our paint database was in 2024, with 2 shades of red paint named Cardinal Red and Red Alert.

In our database we found 31 offerings of Red in 21 of the 21 production years of the Titan (2004-2024). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
