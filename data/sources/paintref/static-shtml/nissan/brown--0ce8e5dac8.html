Brown Nissan Titans at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Nissan Titan in our paint database was in 2004, with 2 brown paint shades named Copper and Sedona.

The last offering of Brown on a Nissan Titan in our paint database was in 2024, with one shade of brown paint named Baja Storm.

In our database we found 22 offerings of Brown in 15 of the 15 production years of the Titan (2004-2005 and 2008-2009 and 2013-2017 and 2019-2024). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
