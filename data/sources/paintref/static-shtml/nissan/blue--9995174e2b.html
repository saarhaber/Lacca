Blue Nissan Maximas at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Nissan Maxima in our paint database was in 1982, with 2 blue paint shades named Light Blue and Pale Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Nissan Maxima in our paint database was in 2023, with one shade of blue paint named Deep Blue.

In our database we found 58 offerings of Blue in 42 of the 42 production years of the Maxima (1982-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
