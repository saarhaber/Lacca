Red Nissan Quests at PaintRef.com


The first Red Nissan Quest in our paint database was in 1993, with one red paint shade named Wild Cherry.

The last offering of Red on a Nissan Quest in our paint database was in 2017, with one shade of red paint named Cayenne Red.

In our database we found 18 offerings of Red in 17 of the 17 production years of the Quest (1993-2002 and 2004-2009 and 2017). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
