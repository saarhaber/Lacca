White Nissan Pathfinders at PaintRef.com


The first White Nissan Pathfinder in our paint database was in 1988, with one white paint shade named Mint White.

The last offering of White on a Nissan Pathfinder in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of white paint named Everest White and Glacier White.

In our database we found 52 offerings of White in 37 of the 37 production years of the Pathfinder (1988-2020 and 2022-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
