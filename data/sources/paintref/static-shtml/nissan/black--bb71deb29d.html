Black Nissan Rogues at PaintRef.com


The first Black Nissan Rogue in our paint database was in 2008, with one black paint shade named Super Black.

The last offering of Black on a Nissan Rogue in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of black paint named Super Black.

In our database we found 22 offerings of Black in 18 of the 18 production years of the Rogue (2008-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
