Black NXs at PaintRef.com


The first Black NX in our paint database was in 1991, with one black paint shade named Super Black.

The last offering of Black on an NX in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of black paint named Caviar and Obsidian.

In our database we found 20 offerings of Black in 14 of the 14 production years of the NX (1991-1993 and 2015-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
