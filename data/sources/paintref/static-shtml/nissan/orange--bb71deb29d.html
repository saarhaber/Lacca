Orange Nissan Rogues at PaintRef.com


The first Orange Nissan Rogue in our paint database was in 2008, with one orange paint shade named Orange Alloy.

The last offering of Orange on a Nissan Rogue in our paint database was in 2023, with one shade of orange paint named Sunset Drift.

In our database we found 12 offerings of Orange in 10 of the 10 production years of the Rogue (2008-2010 and 2017-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
