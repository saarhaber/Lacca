Gray Nissan Titans at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Nissan Titan in our paint database was in 2004, with 2 gray paint shades named Silver and Smoke.

The last offering of Gray on a Nissan Titan in our paint database was in 2024, with 2 shades of gray paint named Baja Storm and Gun.

In our database we found 48 offerings of Gray in 21 of the 21 production years of the Titan (2004-2024). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
