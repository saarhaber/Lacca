Black Nissan Altimas at PaintRef.com


The first Black Nissan Altima in our paint database was in 1993, with one black paint shade named Super Black.

The last offering of Black on a Nissan Altima in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of black paint named Super Black.

In our database we found 37 offerings of Black in 32 of the 32 production years of the Altima (1993-2005 and 2007-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
