Blue Nissan Sentras at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Nissan Sentra in our paint database was in 1982, with one blue paint shade named Pale Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Nissan Sentra in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of blue paint named Atlantic Gray and Electric Blue.

In our database we found 81 offerings of Blue in 44 of the 44 production years of the Sentra (1982-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
