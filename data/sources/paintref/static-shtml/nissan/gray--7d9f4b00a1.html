Gray Nissan Armadas at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Nissan Armada in our paint database was in 2004, with one gray paint shade named Pewter.

The last offering of Gray on a Nissan Armada in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of gray paint named Brilliant Silver and Gun.

In our database we found 49 offerings of Gray in 22 of the 22 production years of the Armada (2004-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
