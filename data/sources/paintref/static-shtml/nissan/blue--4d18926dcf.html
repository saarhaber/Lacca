Blue Nissan Stanzas at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Nissan Stanza in our paint database was in 1982, with one blue paint shade named Deep Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Nissan Stanza in our paint database was in 1992, with one shade of blue paint named Winter Blue.

In our database we found 22 offerings of Blue in 11 of the 11 production years of the Stanza (1982-1992). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
