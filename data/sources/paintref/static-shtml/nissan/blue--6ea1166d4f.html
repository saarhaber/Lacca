Blue Nissan Pathfinders at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Nissan Pathfinder in our paint database was in 1987, with 2 blue paint shades named Blue Mist and Crystal Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Nissan Pathfinder in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of blue paint named Deep Ocean Blue.

In our database we found 52 offerings of Blue in 37 of the 37 production years of the Pathfinder (1987-2003 and 2005-2020 and 2022-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
