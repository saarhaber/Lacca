Gray Nissan Sentras at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Nissan Sentra in our paint database was in 1982, with one gray paint shade named Diamond Mist.

The last offering of Gray on a Nissan Sentra in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of gray paint named Atlantic Gray and Gun.

In our database we found 89 offerings of Gray in 43 of the 43 production years of the Sentra (1982-1995 and 1997-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on a Sentra in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
