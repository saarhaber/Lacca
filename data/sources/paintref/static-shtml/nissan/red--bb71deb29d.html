Red Nissan Rogues at PaintRef.com


The first Red Nissan Rogue in our paint database was in 2008, with 2 red paint shades named Crimson Routlette and Sonoma Sunset.

The last offering of Red on a Nissan Rogue in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of red paint named Scarlet Ember.

In our database we found 20 offerings of Red in 18 of the 18 production years of the Rogue (2008-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
