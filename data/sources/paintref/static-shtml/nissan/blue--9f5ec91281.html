Blue NXs at PaintRef.com


The first Blue NX in our paint database was in 1991, with one blue paint shade named Winter Blue.

The last offering of Blue on an NX in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of blue paint named Grecian Water and Ultrasonic Blue.

In our database we found 25 offerings of Blue in 13 of the 13 production years of the NX (1991 and 1993 and 2015-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
