Brown Nissan Maximas at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Nissan Maxima in our paint database was in 1982, with 3 brown paint shades named Dark Brown, French Beige and Mission Tan.

The last offering of Brown on a Nissan Maxima in our paint database was in 2018, with one shade of brown paint named China Copper.

In our database we found 46 offerings of Brown in 33 of the 33 production years of the Maxima (1982-1983 and 1985-2008 and 2012-2018). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on a Maxima in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
