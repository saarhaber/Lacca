Gray Nissan Maximas at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Nissan Maxima in our paint database was in 1982, with one gray paint shade named Diamond Mist.

The last offering of Gray on a Nissan Maxima in our paint database was in 2023, with 2 shades of gray paint named Brilliant Silver and Gun.

In our database we found 92 offerings of Gray in 42 of the 42 production years of the Maxima (1982-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on a Maxima in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
