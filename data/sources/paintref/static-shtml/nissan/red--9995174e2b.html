Red Nissan Maximas at PaintRef.com


The first Red Nissan Maxima in our paint database was in 1982, with 3 red paint shades named Burgundy Mist, Cameo Beige and Rallye Red.

The last offering of Red on a Nissan Maxima in our paint database was in 2023, with one shade of red paint named Scarlet Ember.

In our database we found 59 offerings of Red in 40 of the 40 production years of the Maxima (1982-1983 and 1985-1999 and 2001-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
