Blue Nissan Altimas at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Nissan Altima in our paint database was in 1993, with 2 blue paint shades named Blue Jade and Pewter Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Nissan Altima in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of blue paint named Bluestone.

In our database we found 51 offerings of Blue in 31 of the 31 production years of the Altima (1993-2003 and 2005-2013 and 2015-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
