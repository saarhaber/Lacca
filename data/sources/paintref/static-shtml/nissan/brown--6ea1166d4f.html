Brown Nissan Pathfinders at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Nissan Pathfinder in our paint database was in 1990, with 2 brown paint shades named Champagne Beige and Pebble Beige.

The last offering of Brown on a Nissan Pathfinder in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of brown paint named Baja Glass and Baja Storm.

In our database we found 54 offerings of Brown in 33 of the 33 production years of the Pathfinder (1990-2005 and 2007-2017 and 2019-2020 and 2022-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
