White Nissan Maximas at PaintRef.com


The first White Nissan Maxima in our paint database was in 1982, with one white paint shade named Alpine White.

The last offering of White on a Nissan Maxima in our paint database was in 2023, with one shade of white paint named Pearl White.

In our database we found 47 offerings of White in 40 of the 40 production years of the Maxima (1982-1983 and 1985-1986 and 1988-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
