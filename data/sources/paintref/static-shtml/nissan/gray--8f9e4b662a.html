Gray Nissan Xterras at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Nissan Xterra in our paint database was in 2000, with one gray paint shade named Silver Ice.

The last offering of Gray on a Nissan Xterra in our paint database was in 2015, with 2 shades of gray paint named Brilliant Silver and Night Armor.

In our database we found 35 offerings of Gray in 15 of the 15 production years of the Xterra (2000-2001 and 2003-2015). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
