Blue Nissan 300ZXs at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Nissan 300ZX in our paint database was in 1981, with one blue paint shade named Satin Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Nissan 300ZX in our paint database was in 1996, with one shade of blue paint named Sapphire Blue.

In our database we found 22 offerings of Blue in 14 of the 14 production years of the 300ZX (1981 and 1984-1996). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
