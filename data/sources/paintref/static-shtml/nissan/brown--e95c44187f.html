Brown Nissan Muranos at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Nissan Murano in our paint database was in 2003, with 2 brown paint shades named Brown and Light Gold.

The last offering of Brown on a Nissan Murano in our paint database was in 2022, with one shade of brown paint named Mocha Almond.

In our database we found 30 offerings of Brown in 20 of the 20 production years of the Murano (2003-2022). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
