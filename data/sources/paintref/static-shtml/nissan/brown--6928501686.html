Brown Nissan 300ZXs at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Nissan 300ZX in our paint database was in 1981, with one brown paint shade named Light Brown.

The last offering of Brown on a Nissan 300ZX in our paint database was in 1993, with one shade of brown paint named Cocoa Bronze.

In our database we found 14 offerings of Brown in 11 of the 11 production years of the 300ZX (1981-1982 and 1984-1990 and 1992-1993). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on a 300ZX in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
