Green Nissan Pathfinders at PaintRef.com


The first Green Nissan Pathfinder in our paint database was in 1990, with one green paint shade named Safari Green.

The last offering of Green on a Nissan Pathfinder in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of green paint named Obsidian Green.

In our database we found 33 offerings of Green in 23 of the 23 production years of the Pathfinder (1990-2002 and 2005 and 2015-2017 and 2019-2020 and 2022-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
