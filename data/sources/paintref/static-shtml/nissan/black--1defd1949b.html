Black Nissan Quests at PaintRef.com


The first Black Nissan Quest in our paint database was in 1993, with one black paint shade named Ebony.

The last offering of Black on a Nissan Quest in our paint database was in 2017, with 2 shades of black paint named Dark Mahogany and Super Black.

In our database we found 29 offerings of Black in 19 of the 19 production years of the Quest (1993-1995 and 1998-2001 and 2004 and 2006-2009 and 2011-2017). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
