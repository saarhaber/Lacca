Gray Nissan Altimas at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Nissan Altima in our paint database was in 1993, with 3 gray paint shades named Pewter Blue, Platinum and Slate Gray.

The last offering of Gray on a Nissan Altima in our paint database was in 2025, with 3 shades of gray paint named Brilliant Silver, Gray Sky and Gun.

In our database we found 78 offerings of Gray in 33 of the 33 production years of the Altima (1993-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
