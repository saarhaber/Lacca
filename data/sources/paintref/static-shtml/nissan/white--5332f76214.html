White Nissan Jukes at PaintRef.com


The first White Nissan Juke in our paint database was in 2011, with one white paint shade named Ivory.

The last offering of White on a Nissan Juke in our paint database was in 2017, with 2 shades of white paint named Glacier White and Pearl White.

In our database we found 8 offerings of White in 7 of the 7 production years of the Juke (2011-2017). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of White on a Juke in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
