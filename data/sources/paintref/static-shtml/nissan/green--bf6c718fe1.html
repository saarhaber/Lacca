Green Nissan Altimas at PaintRef.com


The first Green Nissan Altima in our paint database was in 1993, with one green paint shade named Blue Jade.

The last offering of Green on a Nissan Altima in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of green paint named Tactical Green.

In our database we found 23 offerings of Green in 20 of the 20 production years of the Altima (1993-2011 and 2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
