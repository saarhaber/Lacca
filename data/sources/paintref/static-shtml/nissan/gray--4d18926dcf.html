Gray Stanzas at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Stanza in our paint database was in 1982, with 4 gray paint shades named Dark Gray, Diamond Mist, Silver and Slate Gray.

The last offering of Gray on a Stanza in our paint database was in 1992, with one shade of gray paint named Charcoal.

In our database we found 23 offerings of Gray in 11 of the 11 production years of the Stanza (1982-1992). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
