Brown Stanzas at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Stanza in our paint database was in 1982, with 5 brown paint shades named Brown, Cinnamon, Dark Brown, French Beige and Mission Tan.

The last offering of Brown on a Stanza in our paint database was in 1992, with one shade of brown paint named Pebble Beige.

In our database we found 27 offerings of Brown in 11 of the 11 production years of the Stanza (1982-1992). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
