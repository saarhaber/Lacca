Red Nissan Stanzas at PaintRef.com


The first Red Nissan Stanza in our paint database was in 1982, with 5 red paint shades named Burgundy Mist, Cameo Beige, Rallye Red, Regal Red and Sunfire.

The last offering of Red on a Nissan Stanza in our paint database was in 1992, with 3 shades of red paint named Aztec Red, Burgundy and Cherry Red.

In our database we found 21 offerings of Red in 11 of the 11 production years of the Stanza (1982-1992). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
