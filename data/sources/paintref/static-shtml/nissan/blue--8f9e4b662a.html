Blue Nissan Xterras at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Nissan Xterra in our paint database was in 2000, with one blue paint shade named Denim Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Nissan Xterra in our paint database was in 2015, with one shade of blue paint named Metallic Blue.

In our database we found 17 offerings of Blue in 14 of the 14 production years of the Xterra (2000-2004 and 2006-2010 and 2012-2015). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
