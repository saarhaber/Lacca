Blue Nissan Kickss at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Nissan Kicks in our paint database was in 2018, with one blue paint shade named Deep Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Nissan Kicks in our paint database was in 2025, with 3 shades of blue paint named Arctic Ice, Deep Blue and Electric Blue.

In our database we found 10 offerings of Blue in 8 of the 8 production years of the Kicks (2018-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
