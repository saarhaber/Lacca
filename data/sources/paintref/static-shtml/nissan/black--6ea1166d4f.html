Black Nissan Pathfinders at PaintRef.com


The first Black Nissan Pathfinder in our paint database was in 1987, with one black paint shade named Thunder Black.

The last offering of Black on a Nissan Pathfinder in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of black paint named Obsidian Green and Super Black.

In our database we found 44 offerings of Black in 37 of the 37 production years of the Pathfinder (1987-2005 and 2007-2020 and 2022-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
