Red Nissan 240SXs at PaintRef.com


The first Red Nissan 240SX in our paint database was in 1989, with 2 red paint shades named Cherry Red and Hot Red.

The last offering of Red on a Nissan 240SX in our paint database was in 1998, with 2 shades of red paint named Deep Fuchsia and Ultra Red.

In our database we found 21 offerings of Red in 10 of the 10 production years of the 240SX (1989-1998). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
