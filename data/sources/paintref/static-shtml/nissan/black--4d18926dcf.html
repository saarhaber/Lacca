Black Stanzas at PaintRef.com


The first Black Stanza in our paint database was in 1982, with 2 black paint shades named Black and Thunder Black.

The last offering of Black on a Stanza in our paint database was in 1992, with one shade of black paint named Super Black.

In our database we found 7 offerings of Black in 6 of the 6 production years of the Stanza (1982 and 1985 and 1989-1992). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
