Green Nissan Xterras at PaintRef.com


The first Green Nissan Xterra in our paint database was in 2000, with one green paint shade named Alpine Green.

The last offering of Green on a Nissan Xterra in our paint database was in 2012, with one shade of green paint named Pop Green.

In our database we found 9 offerings of Green in 8 of the 8 production years of the Xterra (2000-2005 and 2011-2012). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
