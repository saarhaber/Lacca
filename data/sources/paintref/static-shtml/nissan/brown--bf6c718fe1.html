Brown Nissan Altimas at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Nissan Altima in our paint database was in 1993, with 3 brown paint shades named Bronze, Champagne and Golden Champagne.

The last offering of Brown on a Nissan Altima in our paint database was in 2017, with one shade of brown paint named Java.

In our database we found 39 offerings of Brown in 25 of the 25 production years of the Altima (1993-2017). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
