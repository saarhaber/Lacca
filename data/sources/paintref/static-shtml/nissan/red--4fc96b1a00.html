Red Nissan Sentras at PaintRef.com


The first Red Nissan Sentra in our paint database was in 1982, with 5 red paint shades named Burgundy Mist, Cameo Beige, Rallye Red, Regatta Red and Sunfire.

The last offering of Red on a Nissan Sentra in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of red paint named Rosewood and Scarlet Ember.

In our database we found 85 offerings of Red in 44 of the 44 production years of the Sentra (1982-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a Sentra in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
