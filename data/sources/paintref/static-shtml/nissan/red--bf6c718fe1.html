Red Nissan Altimas at PaintRef.com


The first Red Nissan Altima in our paint database was in 1993, with one red paint shade named Ruby Pearl.

The last offering of Red on a Nissan Altima in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of red paint named Carnet Pearl and Scarlet Ember.

In our database we found 52 offerings of Red in 33 of the 33 production years of the Altima (1993-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
