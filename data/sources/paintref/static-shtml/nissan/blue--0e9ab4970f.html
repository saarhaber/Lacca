Blue Nissan Micras at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Nissan Micra in our paint database was in 2015, with 2 blue paint shades named Metallic Blue and Turquoise.

The last offering of Blue on a Nissan Micra in our paint database was in 2020, with 2 shades of blue paint named Echo Grey and Power Blue.

In our database we found 18 offerings of Blue in 6 of the 6 production years of the Micra (2015-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
