Blue Nissan 350Zs at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Nissan 350Z in our paint database was in 2003, with one blue paint shade named Blue Pearl.

The last offering of Blue on a Nissan 350Z in our paint database was in 2009, with one shade of blue paint named San Marino Blue.

In our database we found 9 offerings of Blue in 7 of the 7 production years of the 350Z (2003-2009). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
