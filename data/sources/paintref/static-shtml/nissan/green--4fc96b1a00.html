Green Nissan Sentras at PaintRef.com


The first Green Nissan Sentra in our paint database was in 1989, with one green paint shade named Light Jade Gray.

The last offering of Green on a Nissan Sentra in our paint database was in 2008, with one shade of green paint named Metallic Jade.

In our database we found 24 offerings of Green in 20 of the 20 production years of the Sentra (1989-2008). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
