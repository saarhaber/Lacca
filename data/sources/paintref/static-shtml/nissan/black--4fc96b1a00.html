Black Nissan Sentras at PaintRef.com


The first Black Nissan Sentra in our paint database was in 1982, with one black paint shade named Thunder Black.

The last offering of Black on a Nissan Sentra in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of black paint named Super Black.

In our database we found 45 offerings of Black in 40 of the 40 production years of the Sentra (1982 and 1985 and 1987-2005 and 2007-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Black on a Sentra in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
