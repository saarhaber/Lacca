Green Nissan Maximas at PaintRef.com


The first Green Nissan Maxima in our paint database was in 1989, with one green paint shade named Silver Green.

The last offering of Green on a Nissan Maxima in our paint database was in 2011, with one shade of green paint named Mystic Jade.

In our database we found 22 offerings of Green in 15 of the 15 production years of the Maxima (1989 and 1993-2003 and 2009-2011). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
