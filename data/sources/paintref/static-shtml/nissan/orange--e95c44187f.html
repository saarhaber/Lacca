Orange Nissan Muranos at PaintRef.com


The first Orange Nissan Murano in our paint database was in 2003, with one orange paint shade named Bright Copper.

The last offering of Orange on a Nissan Murano in our paint database was in 2022, with one shade of orange paint named Sunset Drift.

In our database we found 15 offerings of Orange in 14 of the 14 production years of the Murano (2003-2008 and 2015-2022). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
