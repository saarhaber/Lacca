Gray Nissan Pathfinders at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Nissan Pathfinder in our paint database was in 1987, with 2 gray paint shades named Medium Pewter and Platinum.

The last offering of Gray on a Nissan Pathfinder in our paint database was in 2025, with 5 shades of gray paint named Baja Glass, Baja Storm, Boulder Gray, Brilliant Silver and Gun.

In our database we found 85 offerings of Gray in 37 of the 37 production years of the Pathfinder (1987-1995 and 1997-2020 and 2022-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
