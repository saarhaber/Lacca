Brown Nissan 240SXs at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Nissan 240SX in our paint database was in 1989, with one brown paint shade named Champagne Beige.

The last offering of Brown on a Nissan 240SX in our paint database was in 1996, with one shade of brown paint named Champagne Gold.

In our database we found 8 offerings of Brown in 8 of the 8 production years of the 240SX (1989-1996). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
