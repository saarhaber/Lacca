Blue Nissan Versas at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Nissan Versa in our paint database was in 2007, with 2 blue paint shades named Dark Blue and New Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Nissan Versa in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of blue paint named Arctic Ice.

In our database we found 40 offerings of Blue in 19 of the 19 production years of the Versa (2007-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
