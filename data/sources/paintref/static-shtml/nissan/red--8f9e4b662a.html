Red Nissan Xterras at PaintRef.com


The first Red Nissan Xterra in our paint database was in 2000, with one red paint shade named Aztec Red.

The last offering of Red on a Nissan Xterra in our paint database was in 2015, with 2 shades of red paint named Cayenne Red and Lava Red.

In our database we found 24 offerings of Red in 16 of the 16 production years of the Xterra (2000-2015). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
