Blue BMW M4s at PaintRef.com


The first Blue BMW M4 in our paint database was in 2014, with one blue paint shade named Interlagos Blau.

The latest offering of Blue on a BMW M4 is available now in the 2026 model year, with 3 shades of blue paint named Frozen Portimao Blue, Portimao Blue and Tanzanite Blue.

In our database we found 42 offerings of Blue in 13 of the 13 production years of the M4 (2014-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
