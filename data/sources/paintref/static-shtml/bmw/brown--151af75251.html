Brown BMW 3 Seriess at PaintRef.com


The first Brown BMW 3 Series in our paint database was in 2000, with 7 brown paint shades named Bronze, Kastanienbraun, Korall, Light Yellow, Messing, Peach and Sahara Beige.

The last offering of Brown on a BMW 3 Series in our paint database was in 2020, with one shade of brown paint named Vermont Bronze.

In our database we found 46 offerings of Brown in 19 of the 19 production years of the 3 Series (2000-2002 and 2005-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
