Green BMW X5s at PaintRef.com


The first Green BMW X5 in our paint database was in 2000, with 3 green paint shades named Oliv, Oxfordgrun and Vermont Green.

The latest offering of Green on a BMW X5 is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of green paint named Manhattan Green.

In our database we found 31 offerings of Green in 18 of the 18 production years of the X5 (2000-2010 and 2020-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
