Black BMW M6s at PaintRef.com


The first Black BMW M6 in our paint database was in 2000, with one black paint shade named Cosmos Black.

The last offering of Black on a BMW M6 in our paint database was in 2019, with 5 shades of black paint named Black Sapphire, Citrin Black, Frozen Black, Gray Black and Ruby Black.

In our database we found 41 offerings of Black in 15 of the 15 production years of the M6 (2000 and 2002 and 2007-2019). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
