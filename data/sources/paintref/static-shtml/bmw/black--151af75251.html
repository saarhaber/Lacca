Black BMW 3 Seriess at PaintRef.com


The first Black BMW 3 Series in our paint database was in 2000, with 2 black paint shades named Carbon Black and Cosmos Black.

The latest offering of Black on a BMW 3 Series is available now in the 2026 model year, with 2 shades of black paint named Black Sapphire and Jet Black.

In our database we found 58 offerings of Black in 27 of the 27 production years of the 3 Series (2000-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
