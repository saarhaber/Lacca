Gray BMW I8s at PaintRef.com


The first Gray BMW I8 in our paint database was in 2015, with 4 gray paint shades named Dark Graphite, Frozen Gray, Ionic Silver and Protonic Blue.

The last offering of Gray on a BMW I8 in our paint database was in 2020, with 3 shades of gray paint named Donington Gray, Frozen Gray and Sophisto Gray.

In our database we found 21 offerings of Gray in 6 of the 6 production years of the I8 (2015-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
