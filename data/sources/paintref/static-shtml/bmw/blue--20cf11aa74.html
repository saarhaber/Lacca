Blue BMW B7s at PaintRef.com


The first Blue BMW B7 in our paint database was in 2011, with 3 blue paint shades named Deep Green, Deep Sea Blue and Imperial Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a BMW B7 in our paint database was in 2022, with 2 shades of blue paint named Alpina Blue and Frozen Bluestone.

In our database we found 30 offerings of Blue in 10 of the 10 production years of the B7 (2011-2017 and 2020-2022). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
