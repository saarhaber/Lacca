White BMW M6s at PaintRef.com


The first White BMW M6 in our paint database was in 2007, with one white paint shade named Alpine White.

The last offering of White on a BMW M6 in our paint database was in 2019, with 3 shades of white paint named Alpine White, Frozen Brilliant White and Imola Red.

In our database we found 18 offerings of White in 12 of the 12 production years of the M6 (2007-2012 and 2014-2019). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
