Red BMW 3 Seriess at PaintRef.com


The first Red BMW 3 Series in our paint database was in 2000, with 9 red paint shades named Bright Red, Canyon Red, Electric Red, Flamenco Red, Imola Red, Lipstick Red, Sienna Red, Sierra Red and Ziegelrot.

The latest offering of Red on a BMW 3 Series is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of red paint named Vegas Red.

In our database we found 57 offerings of Red in 27 of the 27 production years of the 3 Series (2000-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
