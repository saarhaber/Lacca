Green Ford F650s at PaintRef.com


The first Green Ford F650 in our paint database was in 2004, with 4 green paint shades named Aspen Green, Dark Green Satin, Estate Green and Highland Green.

The last offering of Green on a Ford F650 in our paint database was in 2022, with one shade of green paint named Green Gem.

In our database we found 10 offerings of Green in 7 of the 7 production years of the F650 (2004 and 2016-2017 and 2019-2022). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Green on an F650 in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
