Gray BMW M3s at PaintRef.com


The first Gray BMW M3 in our paint database was in 2000, with 2 gray paint shades named Silver Stone and Steel Blue.

Gray is available now on the 2026 BMW M3, with 4 shades of gray paint named Brooklyn Grey, Dravit Grey, Frozen Pure Grey and Skyscraper Grey.

The latest offering of Gray on a BMW M3 will be available in the 2027 model year, with at least 4 shades of gray paint named Brooklyn Grey, Dravit Grey, Frozen Pure Grey and Skyscraper Grey.

In our database we found 73 offerings of Gray in 23 of the 23 production years of the M3 (2000-2006 and 2009-2013 and 2015-2018 and 2021-2027). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
