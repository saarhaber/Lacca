Blue BMW 3 Seriess at PaintRef.com


The first Blue BMW 3 Series in our paint database was in 2000, with 16 blue paint shades named Atlantis Blue, Avus Blue, Barbados Green, Lemans Blue, Maledivenblau, Montreal Blue, Orient Blue, Orinoco, Oxford Green, Santorin Blue, Steel Blue, Titanicblau, Topaz Blue, Tuerkis, Tuerkisgruen and Velvet Blue.

The latest offering of Blue on a BMW 3 Series is available now in the 2026 model year, with 4 shades of blue paint named Arctic Race Blue, Frozen Portimao Blue, Portimao Blue and Tanzanite Blue.

In our database we found 122 offerings of Blue in 27 of the 27 production years of the 3 Series (2000-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a 3 Series in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
