Red BMW Z4s at PaintRef.com


The first Red BMW Z4 in our paint database was in 2003, with 2 red paint shades named Bright Red and Merlot Red.

The latest offering of Red on a BMW Z4 is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of red paint named San Francisco Red.

In our database we found 34 offerings of Red in 23 of the 23 production years of the Z4 (2003-2017 and 2019-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
