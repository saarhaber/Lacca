Red BMW M3s at PaintRef.com


The first Red BMW M3 in our paint database was in 2000, with 2 red paint shades named Bright Red and Sierra Red.

Red is available now on the 2026 BMW M3, with one shade of red paint named Vegas Red.

The latest offering of Red on a BMW M3 will be available in the 2027 model year, with at least 2 shades of red paint named Aventurin Red and Toronto Red.

In our database we found 33 offerings of Red in 23 of the 23 production years of the M3 (2000-2006 and 2009-2013 and 2015-2018 and 2021-2027). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on an M3 in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
