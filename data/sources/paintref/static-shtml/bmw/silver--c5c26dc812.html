Silver BMW M5s at PaintRef.com


The first Silver BMW M5 in our paint database was in 2000, with 3 silver paint shades named Arctic Silver, Silver Stone and Titanium Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a BMW M5 in our paint database was in 2019, with 3 shades of silver paint named Frozen Cashmere Silver, Frozen Dark Silver and Rhodonite Silver.

In our database we found 46 offerings of Silver in 17 of the 17 production years of the M5 (2000-2003 and 2005-2011 and 2013-2016 and 2018-2019). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Silver on an M5 in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
