Blue BMW M5s at PaintRef.com


The first Blue BMW M5 in our paint database was in 2000, with 5 blue paint shades named Avus Blue, Dark Blue, Lemans Blue, Orient Blue and Steel Blue.

The latest offering of Blue on a BMW M5 is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of blue paint named Marina Bay Blue.

In our database we found 66 offerings of Blue in 24 of the 24 production years of the M5 (2000-2003 and 2005-2011 and 2013-2023 and 2025-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
