Silver BMW 3 Seriess at PaintRef.com


The first Silver BMW 3 Series in our paint database was in 2000, with 2 silver paint shades named Aspen Silver and Titanium Silver.

The latest offering of Silver on a BMW 3 Series is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of silver paint named Mineral White.

In our database we found 57 offerings of Silver in 27 of the 27 production years of the 3 Series (2000-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
