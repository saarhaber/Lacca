Silver BMW I3s at PaintRef.com


The first Silver BMW I3 in our paint database was in 2015, with 2 silver paint shades named Andesit Silver and Ionic Silver.

The latest offering of Silver on a BMW I3 will be available in the 2027 model year, with at least 2 shades of silver paint named Mineral White and Space Silver.

In our database we found 10 offerings of Silver in 5 of the 5 production years of the I3 (2015-2017 and 2021 and 2027). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Silver on an I3 in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
