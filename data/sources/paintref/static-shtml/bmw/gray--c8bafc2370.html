Gray BMW X3s at PaintRef.com


The first Gray BMW X3 in our paint database was in 2000, with one gray paint shade named Arctic Silver.

The latest offering of Gray on a BMW X3 is available now in the 2026 model year, with 6 shades of gray paint named Brooklyn Grey, Dark Graphite, Dune Grey, Frozen Deep Grey, Frozen Pure Grey and Skyscraper Grey.

In our database we found 69 offerings of Gray in 23 of the 23 production years of the X3 (2000 and 2005-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
