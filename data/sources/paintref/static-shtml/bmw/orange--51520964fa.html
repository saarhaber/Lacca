Orange BMW M6s at PaintRef.com


The first Orange BMW M6 in our paint database was in 2013, with one orange paint shade named Sakhir Orange.

The last offering of Orange on a BMW M6 in our paint database was in 2019, with 2 shades of orange paint named Fire Orange and Valencia Orange.

In our database we found 10 offerings of Orange in 7 of the 7 production years of the M6 (2013-2019). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
