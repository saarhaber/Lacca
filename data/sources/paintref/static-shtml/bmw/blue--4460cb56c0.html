Blue BMW M8s at PaintRef.com


The first Blue BMW M8 in our paint database was in 2020, with 5 blue paint shades named Barcelona Blue, Frozen Bluestone, Marina Bay Blue, Sonic Speed Blue and Tanzanite Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a BMW M8 in our paint database was in 2025, with 3 shades of blue paint named Frozen Tanzanite Blue, Marina Bay Blue and Tanzanite Blue.

In our database we found 24 offerings of Blue in 6 of the 6 production years of the M8 (2020-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
