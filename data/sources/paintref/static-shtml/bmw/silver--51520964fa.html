Silver BMW M6s at PaintRef.com


The first Silver BMW M6 in our paint database was in 2000, with 2 silver paint shades named Arctic Silver and Titanium Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a BMW M6 in our paint database was in 2019, with 5 shades of silver paint named Frozen Cashmere Silver, Frozen Silver, Moonstone, Pure Metal Silver and Silverstone.

In our database we found 38 offerings of Silver in 14 of the 14 production years of the M6 (2000 and 2002 and 2007-2011 and 2013-2019). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
