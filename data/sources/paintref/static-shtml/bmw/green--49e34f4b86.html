Green BMW M3s at PaintRef.com


The first Green BMW M3 in our paint database was in 2000, with 3 green paint shades named Evergreen, Fern Green and Meergruen.

The latest offering of Green on a BMW M3 will be available in the 2027 model year, with at least one shade of green paint named Isle of Man Green.

In our database we found 25 offerings of Green in 21 of the 21 production years of the M3 (2000-2004 and 2009-2018 and 2021-2025 and 2027). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Green on an M3 in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
