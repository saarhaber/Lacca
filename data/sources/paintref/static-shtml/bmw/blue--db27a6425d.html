Blue BMW Z4s at PaintRef.com


The first Blue BMW Z4 in our paint database was in 2000, with one blue paint shade named Estoril Blau.

The latest offering of Blue on a BMW Z4 is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of blue paint named Portimao Blue.

In our database we found 39 offerings of Blue in 25 of the 25 production years of the Z4 (2000 and 2002-2017 and 2019-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
