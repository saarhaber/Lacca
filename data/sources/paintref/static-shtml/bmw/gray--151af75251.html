Gray BMW 3 Seriess at PaintRef.com


The first Gray BMW 3 Series in our paint database was in 2000, with 8 gray paint shades named Aspen Silver, Fjord Gray, Orinocco, Poetic Light, Steel Blue, Steel Gray, Stratus Gray and Titanium Silver.

The latest offering of Gray on a BMW 3 Series is available now in the 2026 model year, with 4 shades of gray paint named Brooklyn Grey, Dravit Grey, Frozen Pure Grey and Skyscraper Grey.

In our database we found 111 offerings of Gray in 27 of the 27 production years of the 3 Series (2000-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
