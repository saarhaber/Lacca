Green BMW 3 Seriess at PaintRef.com


The first Green BMW 3 Series in our paint database was in 2000, with 11 green paint shades named Barbados Green, Dark Green, Evergreen, Fern Green, Glacier Green, Meergruen, Oxford Green, Scarabaeusguen, Taiga Green, Tuerkis and Tuerkisgruen.

The last offering of Green on a BMW 3 Series in our paint database was in 2024, with one shade of green paint named Isle of Man Green.

In our database we found 35 offerings of Green in 15 of the 15 production years of the 3 Series (2000-2012 and 2022 and 2024). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Green on a 3 Series in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
