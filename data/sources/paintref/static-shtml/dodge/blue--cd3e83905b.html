Blue Dodge Caravans at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Dodge Caravan in our paint database was in 1990, with 2 blue paint shades named Ice Blue and Twilight Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Dodge Caravan in our paint database was in 2011, with one shade of blue paint named Blackberry.

In our database we found 49 offerings of Blue in 21 of the 21 production years of the Caravan (1990-1995 and 1997-2011). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
