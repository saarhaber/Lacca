Gray Dodge Avengers at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Dodge Avenger in our paint database was in 1995, with 2 gray paint shades named Gray and Silver Mist.

The last offering of Gray on a Dodge Avenger in our paint database was in 2014, with 2 shades of gray paint named Billet Silver and Granite Crystal.

In our database we found 26 offerings of Gray in 13 of the 13 production years of the Avenger (1995-1998 and 2000-2001 and 2008-2014). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
