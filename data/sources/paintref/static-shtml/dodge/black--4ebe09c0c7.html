Black Dodge Challengers at PaintRef.com


The first Black Dodge Challenger in our paint database was in 1970, with one black paint shade named Black.

The last offering of Black on a Dodge Challenger in our paint database was in 2023, with one shade of black paint named Pitch Black.

In our database we found 27 offerings of Black in 24 of the 24 production years of the Challenger (1970-1974 and 1979-1981 and 1983 and 2008-2011 and 2013-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
