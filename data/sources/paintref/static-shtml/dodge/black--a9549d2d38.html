The first Black Dodge Caliber in our paint database was in 2007, with one black paint shade named Black.

The last offering of Black on a Dodge Caliber in our paint database was in 2012, with one shade of black paint named Black.

In our database we found 7 offerings of Black in 6 of the 6 production years of the Caliber (2007-2012). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
