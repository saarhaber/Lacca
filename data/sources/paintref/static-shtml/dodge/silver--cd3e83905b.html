Silver Dodge Caravans at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Dodge Caravan in our paint database was in 1990, with 2 silver paint shades named Radiant Silver and Warm Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a Dodge Caravan in our paint database was in 2011, with one shade of silver paint named Bright Silver.

In our database we found 19 offerings of Silver in 17 of the 17 production years of the Caravan (1990-1992 and 1997 and 1999-2011). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
