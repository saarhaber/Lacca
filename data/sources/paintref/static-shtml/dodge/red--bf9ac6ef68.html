Red Dodge Avengers at PaintRef.com


The first Red Dodge Avenger in our paint database was in 1995, with 2 red paint shades named Radiant Red and Wildberry.

The last offering of Red on a Dodge Avenger in our paint database was in 2014, with 2 shades of red paint named Deep Cherry Red and Redline Red.

In our database we found 27 offerings of Red in 14 of the 14 production years of the Avenger (1995-2001 and 2008-2014). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
