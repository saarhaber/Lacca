Blue Dodge Avengers at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Dodge Avenger in our paint database was in 1995, with one blue paint shade named Medium Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Dodge Avenger in our paint database was in 2014, with 2 shades of blue paint named Blue Streak and True Blue.

In our database we found 20 offerings of Blue in 13 of the 13 production years of the Avenger (1995-2001 and 2008-2010 and 2012-2014). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
