Green Dodge Challengers at PaintRef.com


The first Green Dodge Challenger in our paint database was in 1970, with 4 green paint shades named Dark Green, Green Go, Light Green and Sublime.

The last offering of Green on a Dodge Challenger in our paint database was in 2023, with 2 shades of green paint named F8 Green and Sublime.

In our database we found 33 offerings of Green in 17 of the 17 production years of the Challenger (1970-1974 and 1978 and 1980 and 2008 and 2011 and 2015 and 2017-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
