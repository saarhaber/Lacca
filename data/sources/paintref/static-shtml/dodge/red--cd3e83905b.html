Red Dodge Caravans at PaintRef.com


The first Red Dodge Caravan in our paint database was in 1990, with 2 red paint shades named Black Cherry and Claret Red.

The last offering of Red on a Dodge Caravan in our paint database was in 2011, with one shade of red paint named Deep Cherry.

In our database we found 48 offerings of Red in 22 of the 22 production years of the Caravan (1990-2011). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
