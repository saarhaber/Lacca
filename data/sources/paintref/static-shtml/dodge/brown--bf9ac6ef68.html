Brown Dodge Avengers at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Dodge Avenger in our paint database was in 1995, with one brown paint shade named Sand Chargold.

The last offering of Brown on a Dodge Avenger in our paint database was in 2010, with one shade of brown paint named White Gold.

In our database we found 12 offerings of Brown in 10 of the 10 production years of the Avenger (1995-2001 and 2008-2010). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
