Green Pontiac GTOs at PaintRef.com


The first Green Pontiac GTO in our paint database was in 1964, with one green paint shade named Pinehurst Green.

The last offering of Green on a Pontiac GTO in our paint database was in 1974, with 4 shades of green paint named Fernmist Yellow, Lakemist Green, Limefire Green and Pinemist Green.

In our database we found 35 offerings of Green in 11 of the 11 production years of the GTO (1964-1974). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
