Black Pontiac Firebirds at PaintRef.com


The first Black Pontiac Firebird was introduced in 1967, with one paint shade named Starlight Black.

The last offering of Black on a Pontiac Firebird in our paint database was in 2001, with one shade of black paint named Black.

In our database we found 34 offerings of Black in 30 of the 30 production years of the Firebird (1967-1969 and 1971 and 1976-2001). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Black on a Firebird in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
