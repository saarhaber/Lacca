Purple Pontiac GTOs at PaintRef.com


The first Purple Pontiac GTO in our paint database was in 1965, with one purple paint shade named Iris Mist.

The last offering of Purple on a Pontiac GTO in our paint database was in 1967, with one shade of purple paint named Plum Mist.

In our database we found 2 offerings of Purple in 2 of the 2 production years of the GTO (1965 and 1967). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
