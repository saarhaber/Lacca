White Pontiac GTOs at PaintRef.com


The first White Pontiac GTO in our paint database was in 1964, with one white paint shade named Cameo Ivory.

The last offering of White on a Pontiac GTO in our paint database was in 1974, with one shade of white paint named Cameo White.

In our database we found 10 offerings of White in 10 of the 10 production years of the GTO (1964-1969 and 1971-1974). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
