Red Pontiac Grand Prixs at PaintRef.com


The first Red Pontiac Grand Prix in our paint database was in 2000, with 2 red paint shades named Bright Red and Redfire.

The last offering of Red on a Pontiac Grand Prix in our paint database was in 2007, with 2 shades of red paint named Cobalt Red and Red Jewel.

In our database we found 18 offerings of Red in 8 of the 8 production years of the Grand Prix (2000-2007). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
