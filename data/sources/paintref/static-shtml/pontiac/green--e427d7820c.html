Green Pontiac Firebirds at PaintRef.com


The first Green Pontiac Firebird was introduced in 1967, with 4 paint shades named Gulf Turquoise, Linden Green, Mariner Turquoise and Verdoro Green.

The last offering of Green on a Pontiac Firebird in our paint database was in 1999, with one shade of green paint named Bright Green.

In our database we found 47 offerings of Green in 22 of the 22 production years of the Firebird (1967-1978 and 1982 and 1991-1999). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Green on a Firebird in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
