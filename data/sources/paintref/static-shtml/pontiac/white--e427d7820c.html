White Pontiac Firebirds at PaintRef.com


The first White Pontiac Firebird was introduced in 1967, with one paint shade named Cameo Ivory.

The last offering of White on a Pontiac Firebird in our paint database was in 2002, with one shade of white paint named Arctic White.

In our database we found 38 offerings of White in 36 of the 36 production years of the Firebird (1967-2002). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
