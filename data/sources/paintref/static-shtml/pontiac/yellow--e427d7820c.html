Yellow Pontiac Firebirds at PaintRef.com


The first Yellow Pontiac Firebird was introduced in 1967, with 2 paint shades named Mayfair Maize and Montego Cream.

The last offering of Yellow on a Pontiac Firebird in our paint database was in 2002, with one shade of yellow paint named Yellow.

In our database we found 36 offerings of Yellow in 24 of the 24 production years of the Firebird (1967-1970 and 1972-1981 and 1984-1988 and 1992-1995 and 2002). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
