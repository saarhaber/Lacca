Purple Pontiac Firebirds at PaintRef.com


The first Purple Pontiac Firebird was introduced in 1967, with one paint shade named Plum Mist.

The last offering of Purple on a Pontiac Firebird in our paint database was in 1998, with one shade of purple paint named Bright Purple.

In our database we found 8 offerings of Purple in 8 of the 8 production years of the Firebird (1967 and 1989 and 1993-1998). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Purple on a Firebird in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
