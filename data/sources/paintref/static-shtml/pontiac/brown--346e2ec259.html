Brown Pontiac GTOs at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Pontiac GTO in our paint database was in 1964, with 3 brown paint shades named Alamo Beige, Saddle Bronze and Singapore Gold.

The last offering of Brown on a Pontiac GTO in our paint database was in 1974, with 6 shades of brown paint named Carmel Beige, Colonial Gold, Crestwood Brown, Denver Gold, Fire Coral Bronze and Shadowmist Brown.

In our database we found 38 offerings of Brown in 11 of the 11 production years of the GTO (1964-1974). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
