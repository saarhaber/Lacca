Blue Pontiac GTOs at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Pontiac GTO in our paint database was in 1964, with 5 blue paint shades named Aquamarine, Gulfstream Aqua, Nocturne Blue, Skyline Blue and Yorktown Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Pontiac GTO in our paint database was in 2006, with one shade of blue paint named Impulse Blue.

In our database we found 51 offerings of Blue in 14 of the 14 production years of the GTO (1964-1974 and 2004-2006). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
