Brown Pontiac Sunfires at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Pontiac Sunfire in our paint database was in 2000, with 2 brown paint shades named Gold and Light Sandrift.

The last offering of Brown on a Pontiac Sunfire in our paint database was in 2005, with one shade of brown paint named Cashmere.

In our database we found 10 offerings of Brown in 6 of the 6 production years of the Sunfire (2000-2005). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on a Sunfire in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
