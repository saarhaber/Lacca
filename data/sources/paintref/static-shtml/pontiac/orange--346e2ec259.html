Orange Pontiac GTOs at PaintRef.com


The first Orange Pontiac GTO in our paint database was in 1966, with one orange paint shade named Copper Blaze.

The last offering of Orange on a Pontiac GTO in our paint database was in 2006, with one shade of orange paint named Brazen Orange.

In our database we found 6 offerings of Orange in 6 of the 6 production years of the GTO (1966 and 1969-1972 and 2006). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
