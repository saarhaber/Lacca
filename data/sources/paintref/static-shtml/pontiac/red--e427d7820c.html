Red Pontiac Firebirds at PaintRef.com


The first Red Pontiac Firebird was introduced in 1967, with 3 paint shades named Burgundy, Regimental Red and Sierra Red.

The last offering of Red on a Pontiac Firebird in our paint database was in 2002, with 2 shades of red paint named Bright Red and Maple Red.

In our database we found 75 offerings of Red in 36 of the 36 production years of the Firebird (1967-2002). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a Firebird in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
