Blue Pontiac Firebirds at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Pontiac Firebird was introduced in 1967, with 6 paint shades named Blue Charcoal, Fathom Blue, Gulf Turquoise, Mariner Turquoise, Montreux Blue and Tyrol Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Pontiac Firebird in our paint database was in 2002, with 2 shades of blue paint named Blue Green Chameleon and Navy Blue.

In our database we found 95 offerings of Blue in 36 of the 36 production years of the Firebird (1967-2002). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
