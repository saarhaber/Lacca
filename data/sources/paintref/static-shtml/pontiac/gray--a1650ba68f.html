Gray G3s at PaintRef.com


The first Gray G3 in our paint database was in 2005, with 2 gray paint shades named Dark Gray and Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a G3 in our paint database was in 2010, with 3 shades of gray paint named Carbon Flash, Cyber Gray and Switchblade Silver.

In our database we found 5 offerings of Gray in 2 of the 2 production years of the G3 (2005 and 2010). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
