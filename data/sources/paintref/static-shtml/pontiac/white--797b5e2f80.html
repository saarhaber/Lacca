White Pontiac Solstices at PaintRef.com


The first White Pontiac Solstice in our paint database was in 2006, with one white paint shade named Olympic White.

The last offering of White on a Pontiac Solstice in our paint database was in 2010, with one shade of white paint named Olympic White.

In our database we found 5 offerings of White in 5 of the 5 production years of the Solstice (2006-2010). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
