Red Pontiac GTOs at PaintRef.com


The first Red Pontiac GTO in our paint database was in 1964, with 3 red paint shades named Grenadier Red, Marimba Red and Sunfire Red.

The last offering of Red on a Pontiac GTO in our paint database was in 2006, with 2 shades of red paint named Spice Red and Torrid Red.

In our database we found 26 offerings of Red in 14 of the 14 production years of the GTO (1964-1974 and 2004-2006). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a GTO in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
