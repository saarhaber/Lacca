Blue Pontiac Sunfires at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Pontiac Sunfire in our paint database was in 2000, with 2 blue paint shades named Dahlia Blue and Indigo Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Pontiac Sunfire in our paint database was in 2005, with 2 shades of blue paint named Arrival Blue and Dark Steel Blue.

In our database we found 8 offerings of Blue in 6 of the 6 production years of the Sunfire (2000-2005). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
