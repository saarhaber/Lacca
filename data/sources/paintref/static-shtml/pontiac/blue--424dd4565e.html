Blue GMC Safaris at PaintRef.com


The first Blue GMC Safari in our paint database was in 1991, with 3 blue paint shades named Black Sapphire, Gray Blue and Light Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a GMC Safari in our paint database was in 2005, with one shade of blue paint named Cadet Blue.

In our database we found 39 offerings of Blue in 15 of the 15 production years of the Safari (1991-2005). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a Safari in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
