Black Pontiac Tempests at PaintRef.com


The first Black Pontiac Tempest in our paint database was in 1961, with one black paint shade named Regent Black.

The last offering of Black on a Pontiac Tempest in our paint database was in 1968, with one shade of black paint named Starlight Black.

In our database we found 8 offerings of Black in 8 of the 8 production years of the Tempest (1961-1968). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
