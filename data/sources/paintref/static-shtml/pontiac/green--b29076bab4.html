Green Pontiac G6s at PaintRef.com


The first Green Pontiac G6 in our paint database was in 2006, with one green paint shade named Emerald Jewel.

The last offering of Green on a Pontiac G6 in our paint database was in 2010, with one shade of green paint named Urbane Moss.

In our database we found 5 offerings of Green in 5 of the 5 production years of the G6 (2006-2010). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Green on a G6 in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
