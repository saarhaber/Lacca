Blue Pontiac Tempests at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Pontiac Tempest in our paint database was in 1961, with 4 blue paint shades named Bristol Blue, Rainier Turquoise, Richelieu Blue and Tradewind Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Pontiac Tempest in our paint database was in 1968, with 5 shades of blue paint named Aegena Blue, Aleutian Blue, Alpine Blue, Meridian Turquoise and Nordic Blue.

In our database we found 41 offerings of Blue in 8 of the 8 production years of the Tempest (1961-1968). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
