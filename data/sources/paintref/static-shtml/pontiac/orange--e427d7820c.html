Orange Pontiac Firebirds at PaintRef.com


The first Orange Pontiac Firebird was introduced in 1967, with one paint shade named Copper Blaze.

The last offering of Orange on a Pontiac Firebird in our paint database was in 2002, with one shade of orange paint named Sunset Orange.

In our database we found 18 offerings of Orange in 18 of the 18 production years of the Firebird (1967 and 1969-1973 and 1975-1977 and 1980-1981 and 1986 and 1988 and 1996-1998 and 2001-2002). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
