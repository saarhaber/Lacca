Blue Pontiac Grand Prixs at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Pontiac Grand Prix in our paint database was in 2000, with 2 blue paint shades named Medium Gulf Blue and Navy Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Pontiac Grand Prix in our paint database was in 2007, with 3 shades of blue paint named Imperial Blue, Lunar Quartz and Stealth Gray.

In our database we found 11 offerings of Blue in 7 of the 7 production years of the Grand Prix (2000-2003 and 2005-2007). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a Grand Prix in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
