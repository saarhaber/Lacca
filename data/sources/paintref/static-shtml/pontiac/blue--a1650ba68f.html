Blue G3s at PaintRef.com


The first Blue G3 in our paint database was in 2005, with 2 blue paint shades named Azure Blue and Dark Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a G3 in our paint database was in 2010, with 5 shades of blue paint named Desert Bloom, Imperial Blue, Kinetic Blue, Misty Lake and Moroccan Blue.

In our database we found 7 offerings of Blue in 2 of the 2 production years of the G3 (2005 and 2010). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
