Gray Pontiac Firebirds at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Pontiac Firebird was introduced in 1967, with 3 paint shades named Blue Charcoal, Silver Turquoise and Silverglaze.

The last offering of Gray on a Pontiac Firebird in our paint database was in 2002, with one shade of gray paint named Pewter.

In our database we found 59 offerings of Gray in 31 of the 31 production years of the Firebird (1967 and 1969-1991 and 1996-2002). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on a Firebird in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
