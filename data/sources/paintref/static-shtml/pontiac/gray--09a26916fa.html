Gray Pontiac Bonnevilles at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Pontiac Bonneville in our paint database was in 2000, with 2 gray paint shades named Dark Bronzemist and Galaxy Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a Pontiac Bonneville in our paint database was in 2005, with 2 shades of gray paint named Light Tarnished Silver and Medium Spiral Gray.

In our database we found 14 offerings of Gray in 6 of the 6 production years of the Bonneville (2000-2005). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on a Bonneville in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
