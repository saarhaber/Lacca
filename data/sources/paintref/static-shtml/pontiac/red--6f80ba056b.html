Red Pontiac Sunfires at PaintRef.com


The first Red Pontiac Sunfire in our paint database was in 2000, with 2 red paint shades named Bright Red and Cayenne Red.

The last offering of Red on a Pontiac Sunfire in our paint database was in 2005, with 2 shades of red paint named Sport Red and Victory Red.

In our database we found 13 offerings of Red in 6 of the 6 production years of the Sunfire (2000-2005). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a Sunfire in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
