Gray Pontiac Grand Prixs at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Pontiac Grand Prix in our paint database was in 2000, with one gray paint shade named Silvermist.

The last offering of Gray on a Pontiac Grand Prix in our paint database was in 2007, with 3 shades of gray paint named Dark Tarnished Silver, Light Tarnished Silver and Stealth Gray.

In our database we found 15 offerings of Gray in 8 of the 8 production years of the Grand Prix (2000-2007). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on a Grand Prix in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
