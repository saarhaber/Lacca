Gray Pontiac Sunfires at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Pontiac Sunfire in our paint database was in 2000, with one gray paint shade named Ultra Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a Pontiac Sunfire in our paint database was in 2005, with one shade of gray paint named Light Tarnished Silver.

In our database we found 12 offerings of Gray in 6 of the 6 production years of the Sunfire (2000-2005). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
