Red Lincoln Continentals at PaintRef.com


The first Red Lincoln Continental in our paint database was in 1995, with 2 red paint shades named Berry and Electric Current Red.

The last offering of Red on a Lincoln Continental in our paint database was in 2020, with 2 shades of red paint named Burgundy Velvet and Red Carpet.

In our database we found 19 offerings of Red in 12 of the 12 production years of the Continental (1995-2002 and 2017-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a Continental in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
