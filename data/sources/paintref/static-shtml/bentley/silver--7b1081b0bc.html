Silver Lincoln Continentals at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Lincoln Continental in our paint database was in 1995, with one silver paint shade named Silver Frost.

The last offering of Silver on a Lincoln Continental in our paint database was in 2020, with one shade of silver paint named Silver Radiance.

In our database we found 14 offerings of Silver in 12 of the 12 production years of the Continental (1995-2002 and 2017-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Silver on a Continental in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
