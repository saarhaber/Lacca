White Lincoln Continentals at PaintRef.com


The first White Lincoln Continental in our paint database was in 1995, with 3 white paint shades named Ivory, Opal and Performance White.

The last offering of White on a Lincoln Continental in our paint database was in 2020, with one shade of white paint named Pristine White.

In our database we found 29 offerings of White in 12 of the 12 production years of the Continental (1995-2002 and 2017-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of White on a Continental in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
