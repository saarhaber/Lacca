Green Lincoln Continentals at PaintRef.com


The first Green Lincoln Continental in our paint database was in 1995, with 2 green paint shades named Deep Jewel Green and Evergreen Frost.

The last offering of Green on a Lincoln Continental in our paint database was in 2017, with one shade of green paint named Jade Green.

In our database we found 15 offerings of Green in 9 of the 9 production years of the Continental (1995-2002 and 2017). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Green on a Continental in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
