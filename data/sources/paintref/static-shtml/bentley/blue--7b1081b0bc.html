Blue Lincoln Continentals at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Lincoln Continental in our paint database was in 1995, with 3 blue paint shades named Dark Portofino Blue, Portofino Blue and Venetian Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Lincoln Continental in our paint database was in 2020, with one shade of blue paint named Blue Diamond.

In our database we found 23 offerings of Blue in 12 of the 12 production years of the Continental (1995-2002 and 2017-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a Continental in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
