Black Lincoln Continentals at PaintRef.com


The first Black Lincoln Continental in our paint database was in 1995, with one black paint shade named Ebony Black.

The last offering of Black on a Lincoln Continental in our paint database was in 2020, with 2 shades of black paint named Black Velvet and Infinite Black.

In our database we found 14 offerings of Black in 12 of the 12 production years of the Continental (1995-2002 and 2017-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Black on a Continental in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
