Brown Lincoln Continentals at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Lincoln Continental in our paint database was in 1995, with one brown paint shade named Pumice.

The last offering of Brown on a Lincoln Continental in our paint database was in 2020, with 2 shades of brown paint named Chroma Caviar Dark Gray and Iced Mocha.

In our database we found 23 offerings of Brown in 12 of the 12 production years of the Continental (1995-2002 and 2017-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on a Continental in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
