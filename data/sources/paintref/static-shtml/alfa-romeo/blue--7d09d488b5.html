Blue Alfa-Romeo Spiders at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Alfa-Romeo Spider in our paint database was in 1998, with one blue paint shade named Blue Victoria.

The last offering of Blue on an Alfa-Romeo Spider in our paint database was in 2008, with one shade of blue paint named Blue Cobalto.

In our database we found 18 offerings of Blue in 11 of the 11 production years of the Spider (1998-2008). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
