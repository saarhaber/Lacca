Green Alfa-Romeo Spiders at PaintRef.com


The first Green Alfa-Romeo Spider in our paint database was in 1991, with one green paint shade named Foglia Green.

The last offering of Green on an Alfa-Romeo Spider in our paint database was in 2008, with one shade of green paint named Verde Racing.

In our database we found 32 offerings of Green in 18 of the 18 production years of the Spider (1991-2008). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
