Gray Alfa-Romeo 164s at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Alfa-Romeo 164 in our paint database was in 1991, with 2 gray paint shades named Anthracite Gray and Argento Silver.

The last offering of Gray on an Alfa-Romeo 164 in our paint database was in 2008, with one shade of gray paint named Nero Pergusa.

In our database we found 35 offerings of Gray in 18 of the 18 production years of the 164 (1991-2008). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
