The first Black Alfa-Romeo Spider in our paint database was in 1991, with one black paint shade named Onyx Black.

The last offering of Black on an Alfa-Romeo Spider in our paint database was in 1994, with one shade of black paint named Onyx Black.

In our database we found 4 offerings of Black in 4 of the 4 production years of the Spider (1991-1994). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
