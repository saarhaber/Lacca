Gray Alfa-Romeo Spiders at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Alfa-Romeo Spider in our paint database was in 1991, with one gray paint shade named Argento Silver.

The last offering of Gray on an Alfa-Romeo Spider in our paint database was in 2010, with one shade of gray paint named Titanio.

In our database we found 51 offerings of Gray in 20 of the 20 production years of the Spider (1991-2010). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
