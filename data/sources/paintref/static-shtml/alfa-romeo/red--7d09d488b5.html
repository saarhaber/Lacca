Red Alfa-Romeo Spiders at PaintRef.com


The first Red Alfa-Romeo Spider in our paint database was in 1991, with one red paint shade named Alfa Red.

The last offering of Red on an Alfa-Romeo Spider in our paint database was in 2008, with 2 shades of red paint named Rosso Brunello and Rosso Miro.

In our database we found 20 offerings of Red in 14 of the 14 production years of the Spider (1991-1995 and 2000-2008). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
