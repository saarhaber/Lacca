Blue GMC Savanas at PaintRef.com


The first Blue GMC Savana in our paint database was in 1999, with one blue paint shade named Cadet Blue.

The latest offering of Blue on a GMC Savana is available now in the 2026 model year, with 3 shades of blue paint named Blue, Dark Ming Blue and Petro Blue.

In our database we found 32 offerings of Blue in 23 of the 23 production years of the Savana (1999-2015 and 2018-2020 and 2023-2024 and 2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a Savana in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
