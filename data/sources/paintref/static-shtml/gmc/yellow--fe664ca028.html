Yellow GMC Sonomas at PaintRef.com


The first Yellow GMC Sonoma in our paint database was in 1994, with one yellow paint shade named Wheatland Yellow.

The last offering of Yellow on a GMC Sonoma in our paint database was in 2004, with one shade of yellow paint named Yellow.

In our database we found 6 offerings of Yellow in 5 of the 5 production years of the Sonoma (1994-1995 and 2002-2004). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
