Red GMC Terrains at PaintRef.com


The first Red GMC Terrain in our paint database was in 2010, with one red paint shade named Merlot Jewel.

Red is available now on the 2026 GMC Terrain, with one shade of red paint named Volcanic Red.

The latest offering of Red on a GMC Terrain will be available in the 2027 model year, with at least one shade of red paint named Volcanic Red.

In our database we found 19 offerings of Red in 18 of the 18 production years of the Terrain (2010-2027). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a Terrain in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
