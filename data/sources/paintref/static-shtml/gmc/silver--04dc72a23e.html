Silver Suburbans at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Suburban in our paint database was in 1991, with 2 silver paint shades named Silver and Ultra Silver.

The latest offering of Silver on a Suburban is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of silver paint named Sterling Gray.

In our database we found 45 offerings of Silver in 31 of the 31 production years of the Suburban (1991-2020 and 2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
