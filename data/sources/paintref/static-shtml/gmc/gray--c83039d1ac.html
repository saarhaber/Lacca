Gray GMC Canyons at PaintRef.com


The first Gray GMC Canyon in our paint database was in 2004, with one gray paint shade named Silver Birch.

The latest offering of Gray on a GMC Canyon is available now in the 2026 model year, with 2 shades of gray paint named Costal Dune and Sterling.

In our database we found 45 offerings of Gray in 20 of the 20 production years of the Canyon (2004-2012 and 2015-2022 and 2024-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on a Canyon in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
