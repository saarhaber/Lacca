Red GMC Canyons at PaintRef.com


The first Red GMC Canyon in our paint database was in 2004, with 2 red paint shades named Cherry Red and Victory Red.

The latest offering of Red on a GMC Canyon is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of red paint named Volcanic Red.

In our database we found 34 offerings of Red in 19 of the 19 production years of the Canyon (2004-2012 and 2015-2021 and 2024-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a Canyon in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
