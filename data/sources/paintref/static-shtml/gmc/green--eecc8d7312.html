Green GMC Sierras at PaintRef.com


The first Green GMC Sierra in our paint database was in 1999, with 2 green paint shades named Dark Gray Green and Meadow Green.

The last offering of Green on a GMC Sierra in our paint database was in 2025, with 3 shades of green paint named Green, Unripened Green and Woodland Green.

In our database we found 26 offerings of Green in 18 of the 18 production years of the Sierra (1999-2007 and 2010-2011 and 2015-2016 and 2021-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Green on a Sierra in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
