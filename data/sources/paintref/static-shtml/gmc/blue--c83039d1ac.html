Blue GMC Canyons at PaintRef.com


The first Blue GMC Canyon in our paint database was in 2004, with one blue paint shade named Indigo Blue.

The latest offering of Blue on a GMC Canyon is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of blue paint named Rapids.

In our database we found 29 offerings of Blue in 17 of the 17 production years of the Canyon (2004-2012 and 2018-2022 and 2024-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a Canyon in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
