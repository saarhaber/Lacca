Black GMC Sierras at PaintRef.com


The first Black GMC Sierra in our paint database was in 1999, with one black paint shade named Black.

The latest offering of Black on a GMC Sierra is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of black paint named Onyx Black.

In our database we found 35 offerings of Black in 28 of the 28 production years of the Sierra (1999-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
