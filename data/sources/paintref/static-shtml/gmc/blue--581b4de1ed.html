Blue GMC Yukons at PaintRef.com


The first Blue GMC Yukon in our paint database was in 1992, with 3 blue paint shades named Bahama Blue, Light French Blue and Medium Dark Teal.

The latest offering of Blue on a GMC Yukon is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of blue paint named Downpour.

In our database we found 49 offerings of Blue in 32 of the 32 production years of the Yukon (1992-2010 and 2014-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a Yukon in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
