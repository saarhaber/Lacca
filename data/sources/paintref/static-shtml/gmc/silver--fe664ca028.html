Silver GMC Sonomas at PaintRef.com


The first Silver GMC Sonoma in our paint database was in 1991, with one silver paint shade named Light Slate.

The last offering of Silver on a GMC Sonoma in our paint database was in 2004, with one shade of silver paint named Pewter.

In our database we found 18 offerings of Silver in 13 of the 13 production years of the Sonoma (1991-1992 and 1994-2004). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
