Orange Suburbans at PaintRef.com


The first Orange Suburban in our paint database was in 1991, with 2 orange paint shades named Tangier Orange and Wheatland Yellow.

The last offering of Orange on a Suburban in our paint database was in 2024, with one shade of orange paint named Wheatland Yellow.

In our database we found 16 offerings of Orange in 8 of the 8 production years of the Suburban (1991-1992 and 1994-1995 and 2001 and 2003 and 2005 and 2024). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
