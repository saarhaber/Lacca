Red GMC Sonomas at PaintRef.com


The first Red GMC Sonoma in our paint database was in 1991, with one red paint shade named Bright Red.

The last offering of Red on a GMC Sonoma in our paint database was in 2004, with 2 shades of red paint named Cherry Red and Victory Red.

In our database we found 30 offerings of Red in 13 of the 13 production years of the Sonoma (1991 and 1993-2004). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a Sonoma in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
