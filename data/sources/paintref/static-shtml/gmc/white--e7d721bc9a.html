White GMC Terrains at PaintRef.com


The first White GMC Terrain in our paint database was in 2010, with one white paint shade named Summit White.

White is available now on the 2026 GMC Terrain, with 2 shades of white paint named Glacier White and Summit White.

The latest offering of White on a GMC Terrain will be available in the 2027 model year, with at least 2 shades of white paint named Glacier White and Summit White.

In our database we found 27 offerings of White in 18 of the 18 production years of the Terrain (2010-2027). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of White on a Terrain in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
