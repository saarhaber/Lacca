Gray GMC Typhoons at PaintRef.com


The first Gray GMC Typhoon in our paint database was in 1992, with 2 gray paint shades named Gray and Light Slate.

The last offering of Gray on a GMC Typhoon in our paint database was in 1993, with 2 shades of gray paint named Dove Gray and Gray.

In our database we found 4 offerings of Gray in 2 of the 2 production years of the Typhoon (1992-1993). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
