Silver GMC Safaris at PaintRef.com


The first Silver GMC Safari in our paint database was in 1991, with one silver paint shade named Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a GMC Safari in our paint database was in 2005, with one shade of silver paint named Pewter.

In our database we found 15 offerings of Silver in 15 of the 15 production years of the Safari (1991-2005). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
