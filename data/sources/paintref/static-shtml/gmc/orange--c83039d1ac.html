Orange GMC Canyons at PaintRef.com


The first Orange GMC Canyon in our paint database was in 2004, with one orange paint shade named Sunburst Orange.

The latest offering of Orange on a GMC Canyon is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of orange paint named Desert Sun.

In our database we found 13 offerings of Orange in 13 of the 13 production years of the Canyon (2004-2010 and 2012 and 2015-2017 and 2019 and 2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Orange on a Canyon in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
