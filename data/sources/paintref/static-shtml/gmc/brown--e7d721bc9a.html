Brown GMC Terrains at PaintRef.com


The first Brown GMC Terrain in our paint database was in 2010, with 2 brown paint shades named Gold Mist and Mocha Steel.

The last offering of Brown on a GMC Terrain in our paint database was in 2024, with one shade of brown paint named Deep Bronze.

In our database we found 14 offerings of Brown in 9 of the 9 production years of the Terrain (2010-2012 and 2014 and 2018-2019 and 2022-2024). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on a Terrain in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
