Silver GMC Yukons at PaintRef.com


The first Silver GMC Yukon in our paint database was in 1992, with one silver paint shade named Ultra Silver.

The latest offering of Silver on a GMC Yukon is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of silver paint named Sterling.

In our database we found 37 offerings of Silver in 31 of the 31 production years of the Yukon (1992-2020 and 2025-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
