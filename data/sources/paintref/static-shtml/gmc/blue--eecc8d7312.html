Blue GMC Sierras at PaintRef.com


The first Blue GMC Sierra in our paint database was in 1999, with one blue paint shade named Indigo.

The latest offering of Blue on a GMC Sierra is available now in the 2026 model year, with 2 shades of blue paint named Deep Ocean and Downpour.

In our database we found 68 offerings of Blue in 28 of the 28 production years of the Sierra (1999-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a Sierra in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
