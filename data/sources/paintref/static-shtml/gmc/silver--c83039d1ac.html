Silver GMC Canyons at PaintRef.com


The first Silver GMC Canyon in our paint database was in 2004, with one silver paint shade named Silver Birch.

The latest offering of Silver on a GMC Canyon is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of silver paint named Sterling.

In our database we found 18 offerings of Silver in 17 of the 17 production years of the Canyon (2004-2012 and 2015-2020 and 2025-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Silver on a Canyon in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
