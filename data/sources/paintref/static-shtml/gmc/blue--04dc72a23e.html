Blue Suburbans at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Suburban in our paint database was in 1991, with 6 blue paint shades named Bahama Blue, Black Sapphire, Bright Blue, Dark Blue, Light Blue and Sky Blue.

The latest offering of Blue on a Suburban is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of blue paint named Lakeshore Blue.

In our database we found 80 offerings of Blue in 35 of the 35 production years of the Suburban (1991-2014 and 2016-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
