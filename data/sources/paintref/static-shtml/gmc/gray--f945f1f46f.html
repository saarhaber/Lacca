Gray Audi S7s at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Audi S7 in our paint database was in 2013, with 3 gray paint shades named Cool Silver, Oolong Gray and Quartz Gray.

The last offering of Gray on an Audi S7 in our paint database was in 2025, with 3 shades of gray paint named Chronos Gray, Daytona Gray and Florett Silver.

In our database we found 35 offerings of Gray in 12 of the 12 production years of the S7 (2013-2018 and 2020-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
