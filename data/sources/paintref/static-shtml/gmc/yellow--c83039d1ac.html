Yellow GMC Canyons at PaintRef.com


The first Yellow GMC Canyon in our paint database was in 2004, with one yellow paint shade named Yellow.

The last offering of Yellow on a GMC Canyon in our paint database was in 2024, with one shade of yellow paint named Solar Flare.

In our database we found 5 offerings of Yellow in 5 of the 5 production years of the Canyon (2004-2007 and 2024). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
