Brown GMC Canyons at PaintRef.com


The first Brown GMC Canyon in our paint database was in 2006, with one brown paint shade named Sandalwood.

The latest offering of Brown on a GMC Canyon is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of brown paint named Costal Dune.

In our database we found 11 offerings of Brown in 10 of the 10 production years of the Canyon (2006 and 2015-2017 and 2019 and 2021-2022 and 2024-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on a Canyon in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
