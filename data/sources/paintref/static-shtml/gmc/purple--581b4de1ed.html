Purple GMC Yukons at PaintRef.com


The first Purple GMC Yukon in our paint database was in 2015, with one purple paint shade named Midnight Amethyst.

The last offering of Purple on a GMC Yukon in our paint database was in 2017, with 2 shades of purple paint named Black Cherry and Midnight Amethyst.

In our database we found 4 offerings of Purple in 3 of the 3 production years of the Yukon (2015-2017). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
