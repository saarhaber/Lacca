Red GMC Sierras at PaintRef.com


The first Red GMC Sierra in our paint database was in 1999, with 2 red paint shades named Dark Toreador and Victory Red.

The latest offering of Red on a GMC Sierra is available now in the 2026 model year, with 3 shades of red paint named Cardinal Red, Dark Ember and Volcanic Red.

In our database we found 62 offerings of Red in 28 of the 28 production years of the Sierra (1999-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a Sierra in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
