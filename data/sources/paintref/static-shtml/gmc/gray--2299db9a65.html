Gray GMC Savanas at PaintRef.com


The first Gray GMC Savana in our paint database was in 1999, with 2 gray paint shades named Dark Bronzemist and Storm Gray.

The latest offering of Gray on a GMC Savana is available now in the 2026 model year, with 3 shades of gray paint named Quicksilver, Silver and Silver Birch.

In our database we found 60 offerings of Gray in 28 of the 28 production years of the Savana (1999-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on a Savana in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
