Gray GMC Terrains at PaintRef.com


The first Gray GMC Terrain in our paint database was in 2010, with 3 gray paint shades named Carbon Black, Cyber Gray and Quicksilver.

Gray is available now on the 2026 GMC Terrain, with 2 shades of gray paint named Riverstone and Sterling.

The latest offering of Gray on a GMC Terrain will be available in the 2027 model year, with at least 2 shades of gray paint named Riverstone and Sterling.

In our database we found 50 offerings of Gray in 18 of the 18 production years of the Terrain (2010-2027). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on a Terrain in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
