Green GMC Yukons at PaintRef.com


The first Green GMC Yukon in our paint database was in 1995, with 2 green paint shades named Dark Woodland Green and Emerald Green.

The last offering of Green on a GMC Yukon in our paint database was in 2024, with one shade of green paint named Frosted Pine.

In our database we found 18 offerings of Green in 15 of the 15 production years of the Yukon (1995-2004 and 2016-2017 and 2021 and 2023-2024). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Green on a Yukon in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
