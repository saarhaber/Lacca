Green Suburbans at PaintRef.com


The first Green Suburban in our paint database was in 1995, with 3 green paint shades named Dark Green, Dark Woodland Green and Emerald Green.

The last offering of Green on a Suburban in our paint database was in 2024, with 2 shades of green paint named Silver Sage and Woodland Green.

In our database we found 27 offerings of Green in 16 of the 16 production years of the Suburban (1995-2005 and 2011 and 2016 and 2019 and 2023-2024). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Green on a Suburban in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
