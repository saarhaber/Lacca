Red GMC Savanas at PaintRef.com


The first Red GMC Savana in our paint database was in 2001, with one red paint shade named Victory Red.

The latest offering of Red on a GMC Savana is available now in the 2026 model year, with 3 shades of red paint named Cardinal Red, Dark Toreador Red and Victory Red.

In our database we found 39 offerings of Red in 26 of the 26 production years of the Savana (2001-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a Savana in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
