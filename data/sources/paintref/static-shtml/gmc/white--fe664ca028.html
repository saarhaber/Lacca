White GMC Sonomas at PaintRef.com


The first White GMC Sonoma in our paint database was in 1991, with one white paint shade named White.

The last offering of White on a GMC Sonoma in our paint database was in 2004, with one shade of white paint named Olympic White.

In our database we found 14 offerings of White in 14 of the 14 production years of the Sonoma (1991-2004). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of White on a Sonoma in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
