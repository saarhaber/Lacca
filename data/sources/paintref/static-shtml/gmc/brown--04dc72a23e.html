Brown Suburbans at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Suburban in our paint database was in 1991, with one brown paint shade named Light Mesa Brown.

The last offering of Brown on a Suburban in our paint database was in 2024, with one shade of brown paint named Empire Beige.

In our database we found 62 offerings of Brown in 29 of the 29 production years of the Suburban (1991-1996 and 1998-2001 and 2003-2016 and 2018 and 2021-2024). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
