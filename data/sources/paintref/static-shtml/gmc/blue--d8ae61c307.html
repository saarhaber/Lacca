Blue GMC Jimmys at PaintRef.com


The first Blue GMC Jimmy in our paint database was in 1991, with 5 blue paint shades named Bahama Blue, Black Sapphire, Bright Blue, Dark Blue and Light Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a GMC Jimmy in our paint database was in 2005, with one shade of blue paint named Indigo Blue.

In our database we found 29 offerings of Blue in 14 of the 14 production years of the Jimmy (1991-1997 and 1999-2005). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a Jimmy in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
