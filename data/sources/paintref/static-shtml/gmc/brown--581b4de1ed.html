Brown GMC Yukons at PaintRef.com


The first Brown GMC Yukon in our paint database was in 1992, with one brown paint shade named Beige.

The last offering of Brown on a GMC Yukon in our paint database was in 2024, with one shade of brown paint named Pearl Beige.

In our database we found 41 offerings of Brown in 27 of the 27 production years of the Yukon (1992-1996 and 1998-2001 and 2003-2014 and 2016-2017 and 2021-2024). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on a Yukon in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
