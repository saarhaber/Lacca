Brown GMC Savanas at PaintRef.com


The first Brown GMC Savana in our paint database was in 1999, with one brown paint shade named Copper.

The latest offering of Brown on a GMC Savana is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of brown paint named Inca Gold.

In our database we found 22 offerings of Brown in 22 of the 22 production years of the Savana (1999-2017 and 2021 and 2024 and 2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on a Savana in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
