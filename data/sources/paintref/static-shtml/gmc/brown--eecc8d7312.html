Brown GMC Sierras at PaintRef.com


The first Brown GMC Sierra in our paint database was in 1999, with 2 brown paint shades named Copper and Gold.

The latest offering of Brown on a GMC Sierra is available now in the 2026 model year, with 2 shades of brown paint named Costal Dune and Deep Aurora.

In our database we found 36 offerings of Brown in 24 of the 24 production years of the Sierra (1999-2001 and 2003-2008 and 2012-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on a Sierra in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
