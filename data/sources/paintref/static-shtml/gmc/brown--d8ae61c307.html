Brown GMC Jimmys at PaintRef.com


The first Brown GMC Jimmy in our paint database was in 1991, with one brown paint shade named Light Mesa Brown.

The last offering of Brown on a GMC Jimmy in our paint database was in 2005, with one shade of brown paint named Sandalwood.

In our database we found 20 offerings of Brown in 13 of the 13 production years of the Jimmy (1991-1992 and 1994-2000 and 2002-2005). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on a Jimmy in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
