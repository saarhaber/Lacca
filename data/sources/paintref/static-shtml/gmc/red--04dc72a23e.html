Red Suburbans at PaintRef.com


The first Red Suburban in our paint database was in 1991, with one red paint shade named Bright Red.

The latest offering of Red on a Suburban is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of red paint named Radiant Red.

In our database we found 59 offerings of Red in 36 of the 36 production years of the Suburban (1991-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
