Red GMC Yukons at PaintRef.com


The first Red GMC Yukon in our paint database was in 1992, with one red paint shade named Dark Garnet Red.

The latest offering of Red on a GMC Yukon is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of red paint named Volcanic Red.

In our database we found 47 offerings of Red in 35 of the 35 production years of the Yukon (1992-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a Yukon in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
