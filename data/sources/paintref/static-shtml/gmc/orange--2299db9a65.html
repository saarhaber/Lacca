Orange GMC Savanas at PaintRef.com


The first Orange GMC Savana in our paint database was in 2012, with one orange paint shade named Wheatland Yellow.

The latest offering of Orange on a GMC Savana is available now in the 2026 model year, with 3 shades of orange paint named Tangier Orange, Wheatland Yellow and Yellow.

In our database we found 15 offerings of Orange in 9 of the 9 production years of the Savana (2012-2017 and 2023-2024 and 2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Orange on a Savana in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
