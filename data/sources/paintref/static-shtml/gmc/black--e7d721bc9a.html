Black GMC Terrains at PaintRef.com


The first Black GMC Terrain in our paint database was in 2010, with 2 black paint shades named Carbon Black and Onyx Black.

Black is available now on the 2026 GMC Terrain, with one shade of black paint named Ebony Twilight.

The latest offering of Black on a GMC Terrain will be available in the 2027 model year, with at least one shade of black paint named Ebony Twilight.

In our database we found 26 offerings of Black in 18 of the 18 production years of the Terrain (2010-2027). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Black on a Terrain in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
