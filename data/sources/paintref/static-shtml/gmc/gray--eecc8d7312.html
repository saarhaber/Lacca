Gray GMC Sierras at PaintRef.com


The first Gray GMC Sierra in our paint database was in 1999, with 5 gray paint shades named Dark Argent, Gray, Pewter, Silver Mist and Storm Gray.

The latest offering of Gray on a GMC Sierra is available now in the 2026 model year, with 5 shades of gray paint named Costal Dune, Magnus Gray Matte, Sterling, Thunderstorm Gray and Titanium Rush.

In our database we found 104 offerings of Gray in 28 of the 28 production years of the Sierra (1999-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on a Sierra in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
