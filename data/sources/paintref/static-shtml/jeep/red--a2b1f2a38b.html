Red Jeep Libertys at PaintRef.com


The first Red Jeep Liberty in our paint database was in 2010, with 2 red paint shades named Caliente Red and Inferno Red.

The last offering of Red on a Jeep Liberty in our paint database was in 2012, with 2 shades of red paint named Deep Cherry Red and Deep Molten Red.

In our database we found 5 offerings of Red in 3 of the 3 production years of the Liberty (2010-2012). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a Liberty in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
