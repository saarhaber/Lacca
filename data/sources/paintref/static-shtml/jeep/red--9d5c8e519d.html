Red Jeep Compasss at PaintRef.com


The first Red Jeep Compass in our paint database was in 2010, with one red paint shade named Inferno Red.

The last offering of Red on a Jeep Compass in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of red paint named Ret Hot.

In our database we found 27 offerings of Red in 16 of the 16 production years of the Compass (2010-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
