Orange Jeep Cherokees at PaintRef.com


The first Orange Jeep Cherokee in our paint database was in 2014, with one orange paint shade named Mango Tango.

The last offering of Orange on a Jeep Cherokee in our paint database was in 2022, with one shade of orange paint named Sunburst.

In our database we found 6 offerings of Orange in 6 of the 6 production years of the Cherokee (2014-2016 and 2020-2022). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Orange on a Cherokee in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
