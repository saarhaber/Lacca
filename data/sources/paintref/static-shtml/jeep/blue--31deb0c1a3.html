Blue Jeep Patriots at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Jeep Patriot in our paint database was in 2010, with one blue paint shade named Deep Water Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Jeep Patriot in our paint database was in 2017, with one shade of blue paint named True Blue.

In our database we found 12 offerings of Blue in 8 of the 8 production years of the Patriot (2010-2017). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
