Gray Jeep Grand Cherokees at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Jeep Grand Cherokee in our paint database was in 2010, with 3 gray paint shades named Bright Silver, Light Graystone and Mineral Gray.

The last offering of Gray on a Jeep Grand Cherokee in our paint database was in 2025, with 3 shades of gray paint named Baltic Gray, Midnight Sky and Silver Zynith.

In our database we found 47 offerings of Gray in 16 of the 16 production years of the Grand Cherokee (2010-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
