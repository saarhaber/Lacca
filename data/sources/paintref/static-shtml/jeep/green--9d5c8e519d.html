Green Jeep Compasss at PaintRef.com


The first Green Jeep Compass in our paint database was in 2010, with 3 green paint shades named Jeep Green, Natural Green and Optic Green.

The last offering of Green on a Jeep Compass in our paint database was in 2021, with one shade of green paint named Olive Green.

In our database we found 14 offerings of Green in 10 of the 10 production years of the Compass (2010-2013 and 2015-2016 and 2018-2021). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
