Gray Jeep Cherokees at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Jeep Cherokee in our paint database was in 2014, with 3 gray paint shades named Anvil, Billet Silver and Granite Crystal.

The last offering of Gray on a Jeep Cherokee in our paint database was in 2023, with 2 shades of gray paint named Granite Crystal and Sting Gray.

In our database we found 29 offerings of Gray in 10 of the 10 production years of the Cherokee (2014-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
