Blue Jeep Cherokees at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Jeep Cherokee in our paint database was in 2011, with one blue paint shade named Deep Water Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Jeep Cherokee in our paint database was in 2023, with one shade of blue paint named Hydro Blue.

In our database we found 19 offerings of Blue in 11 of the 11 production years of the Cherokee (2011 and 2014-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a Cherokee in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
