White Jeep Compasss at PaintRef.com


The first White Jeep Compass in our paint database was in 2010, with one white paint shade named Stone White.

The last offering of White on a Jeep Compass in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of white paint named Bright White.

In our database we found 20 offerings of White in 16 of the 16 production years of the Compass (2010-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
