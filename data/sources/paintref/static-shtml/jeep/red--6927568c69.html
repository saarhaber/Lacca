Red Jeep Cherokees at PaintRef.com


The first Red Jeep Cherokee in our paint database was in 2011, with one red paint shade named Inferno Red.

The last offering of Red on a Jeep Cherokee in our paint database was in 2022, with one shade of red paint named Velvet Red.

In our database we found 17 offerings of Red in 10 of the 10 production years of the Cherokee (2011 and 2014-2022). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a Cherokee in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
