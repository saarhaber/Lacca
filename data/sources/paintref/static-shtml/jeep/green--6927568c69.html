Green Jeep Cherokees at PaintRef.com


The first Green Jeep Cherokee in our paint database was in 2011, with one green paint shade named Natural Green.

The last offering of Green on a Jeep Cherokee in our paint database was in 2021, with one shade of green paint named Olive Green.

In our database we found 9 offerings of Green in 8 of the 8 production years of the Cherokee (2011 and 2014-2016 and 2018-2021). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
