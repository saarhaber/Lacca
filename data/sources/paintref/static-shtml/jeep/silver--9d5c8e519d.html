Silver Jeep Compasss at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Jeep Compass in our paint database was in 2010, with one silver paint shade named Bright Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a Jeep Compass in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of silver paint named Silver Zynith.

In our database we found 16 offerings of Silver in 16 of the 16 production years of the Compass (2010-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
