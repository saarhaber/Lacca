Brown Jeep Gladiators at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Jeep Gladiator in our paint database was in 2020, with 2 brown paint shades named Gator and Gobi.

The last offering of Brown on a Jeep Gladiator in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of brown paint named 41.

In our database we found 4 offerings of Brown in 3 of the 3 production years of the Gladiator (2020 and 2022 and 2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
