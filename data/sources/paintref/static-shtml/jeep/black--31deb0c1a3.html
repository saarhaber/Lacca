Black Jeep Patriots at PaintRef.com


The first Black Jeep Patriot in our paint database was in 2010, with one black paint shade named Brilliant Black.

The last offering of Black on a Jeep Patriot in our paint database was in 2017, with one shade of black paint named Black.

In our database we found 9 offerings of Black in 8 of the 8 production years of the Patriot (2010-2017). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
