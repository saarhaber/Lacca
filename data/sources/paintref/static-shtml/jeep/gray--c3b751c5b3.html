Gray Jeep Gladiators at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Jeep Gladiator in our paint database was in 2020, with 3 gray paint shades named Billet, Granite Crystal and Sting Grey.

The last offering of Gray on a Jeep Gladiator in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of gray paint named Anvil and Granite Crystal.

In our database we found 17 offerings of Gray in 6 of the 6 production years of the Gladiator (2020-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
