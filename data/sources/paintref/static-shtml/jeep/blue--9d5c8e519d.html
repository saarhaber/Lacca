Blue Jeep Compasss at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Jeep Compass in our paint database was in 2010, with one blue paint shade named Deep Water Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Jeep Compass in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of blue paint named Hydro Blue.

In our database we found 25 offerings of Blue in 16 of the 16 production years of the Compass (2010-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
