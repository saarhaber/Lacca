Red Jeep Grand Cherokees at PaintRef.com


The first Red Jeep Grand Cherokee in our paint database was in 2010, with one red paint shade named Red Rock Crystal.

The last offering of Red on a Jeep Grand Cherokee in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of red paint named Velvet Red.

In our database we found 32 offerings of Red in 16 of the 16 production years of the Grand Cherokee (2010-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
