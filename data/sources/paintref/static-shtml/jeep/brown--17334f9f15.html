Brown Jeep Grand Cherokees at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Jeep Grand Cherokee in our paint database was in 2011, with 2 brown paint shades named Bronze Star and White Gold.

The last offering of Brown on a Jeep Grand Cherokee in our paint database was in 2024, with 2 shades of brown paint named Copper Shino and Rocky Mountain.

In our database we found 24 offerings of Brown in 14 of the 14 production years of the Grand Cherokee (2011-2024). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
