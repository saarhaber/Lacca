Gray Jeep Grand Wagoneers at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Jeep Grand Wagoneer in our paint database was in 2022, with 4 gray paint shades named Baltic Gray, Midnight Sky, River Rock and Zynith Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a Jeep Grand Wagoneer in our paint database was in 2025, with 4 shades of gray paint named Baltic Gray, Midnight Sky, River Rock and Silver Zynith.

In our database we found 16 offerings of Gray in 4 of the 4 production years of the Grand Wagoneer (2022-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
