Green Jeep Grand Cherokees at PaintRef.com


The first Green Jeep Grand Cherokee in our paint database was in 2010, with 2 green paint shades named Jeep Green and Optic Green.

The last offering of Green on a Jeep Grand Cherokee in our paint database was in 2020, with one shade of green paint named Green.

In our database we found 10 offerings of Green in 9 of the 9 production years of the Grand Cherokee (2010-2017 and 2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
