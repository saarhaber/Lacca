Red Audi RS7s at PaintRef.com


The first Red Audi RS7 in our paint database was in 2014, with one red paint shade named Misano Red.

The latest offering of Red on an Audi RS7 is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of red paint named Grenadine Red.

In our database we found 10 offerings of Red in 10 of the 10 production years of the RS7 (2014-2015 and 2017-2018 and 2021-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
