Gray Audi S4s at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Audi S4 in our paint database was in 2004, with 2 gray paint shades named Dolphin Gray and Light Silver.

The last offering of Gray on an Audi S4 in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of gray paint named Chronos Gray and Daytona Gray.

In our database we found 53 offerings of Gray in 22 of the 22 production years of the S4 (2004-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
