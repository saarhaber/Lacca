Blue Audi Q3s at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Audi Q3 in our paint database was in 2014, with 3 blue paint shades named Kobaltblau, Sepang Blue and Utopia Blue.

The latest offering of Blue on an Audi Q3 is available now in the 2026 model year, with 2 shades of blue paint named Malpelo Blue and Navarra Blue.

In our database we found 23 offerings of Blue in 12 of the 12 production years of the Q3 (2014-2018 and 2020-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
