Brown Audi A8s at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Audi A8 in our paint database was in 2007, with one brown paint shade named Pearl Beige.

The latest offering of Brown on an Audi A8 is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of brown paint named Madeira Brown.

In our database we found 25 offerings of Brown in 17 of the 17 production years of the A8 (2007-2021 and 2025-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
