Red Audi Q7s at PaintRef.com


The first Red Audi Q7 in our paint database was in 2007, with one red paint shade named Garnet Red.

The latest offering of Red on an Audi Q7 is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of red paint named Chili Red.

In our database we found 9 offerings of Red in 8 of the 8 production years of the Q7 (2007-2009 and 2014 and 2016-2017 and 2022 and 2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
