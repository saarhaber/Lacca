White Audi A7s at PaintRef.com


The first White Audi A7 in our paint database was in 2012, with 2 white paint shades named Glacier White and Ibis White.

The latest offering of White on an Audi A7 is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of white paint named Glacier White.

In our database we found 25 offerings of White in 14 of the 14 production years of the A7 (2012-2019 and 2021-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
