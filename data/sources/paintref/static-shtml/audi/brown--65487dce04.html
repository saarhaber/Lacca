Brown Audi A6s at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Audi A6 in our paint database was in 2004, with one brown paint shade named Canvas Beige.

Brown is available now on the 2026 Audi A6, with one shade of brown paint named Madeira Brown.

The latest offering of Brown on an Audi A6 will be available in the 2027 model year, with at least one shade of brown paint named Siam Beige.

In our database we found 28 offerings of Brown in 22 of the 22 production years of the A6 (2004-2009 and 2012-2027). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
