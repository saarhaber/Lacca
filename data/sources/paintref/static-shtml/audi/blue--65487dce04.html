Blue Audi A6s at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Audi A6 in our paint database was in 2004, with 2 blue paint shades named Crystal Blue and Night Blue.

Blue is available now on the 2026 Audi A6, with 3 shades of blue paint named Ascari Blue, Firmament Blue and Horizon Blue.

The latest offering of Blue on an Audi A6 will be available in the 2027 model year, with at least 2 shades of blue paint named Malpelo Blue and Plasma Blue.

In our database we found 47 offerings of Blue in 24 of the 24 production years of the A6 (2004-2027). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on an A6 in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
