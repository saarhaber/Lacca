Gray Audi R8s at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Audi R8 in our paint database was in 2008, with 3 gray paint shades named Daytona Gray, Ice Silver and Jet Blue.

The last offering of Gray on an Audi R8 in our paint database was in 2023, with 4 shades of gray paint named Daytona Gray, Florett Silver, Kemora Gray and Suzuka Gray.

In our database we found 58 offerings of Gray in 14 of the 14 production years of the R8 (2008-2015 and 2017-2018 and 2020-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
