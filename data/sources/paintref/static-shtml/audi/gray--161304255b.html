Gray Audi Q6s at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Audi Q6 in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 gray paint shades named Magnet Gray and Manhattan Gray.

The latest offering of Gray on an Audi Q6 will be available in the 2027 model year, with at least 2 shades of gray paint named Magnet Gray and Manhattan Gray.

In our database we found 4 offerings of Gray in 2 of the 2 production years of the Q6 (2025 and 2027). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
