Black Audi A6s at PaintRef.com


The first Black Audi A6 in our paint database was in 2004, with one black paint shade named Brilliant Black.

Black is available now on the 2026 Audi A6, with 4 shades of black paint named Brilliant Black, Midnight Green, Mythos Black and Sebring Black Crystal.

The latest offering of Black on an Audi A6 will be available in the 2027 model year, with at least one shade of black paint named Mythos Black.

In our database we found 48 offerings of Black in 23 of the 23 production years of the A6 (2004-2019 and 2021-2027). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
