Gray Audi Q5s at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Audi Q5 in our paint database was in 2010, with 4 gray paint shades named Ice Silver, Lava Gray, Meteor Gray and Quartz Gray.

The latest offering of Gray on an Audi Q5 is available now in the 2026 model year, with 3 shades of gray paint named Daytona Gray, Florett Silver and Tambora Gray.

In our database we found 65 offerings of Gray in 17 of the 17 production years of the Q5 (2010-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
