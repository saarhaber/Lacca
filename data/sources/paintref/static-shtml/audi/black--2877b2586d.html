Black Audi R8s at PaintRef.com


The first Black Audi R8 in our paint database was in 2008, with one black paint shade named Phantom Black.

The last offering of Black on an Audi R8 in our paint database was in 2023, with one shade of black paint named Mythos Black.

In our database we found 17 offerings of Black in 13 of the 13 production years of the R8 (2008-2015 and 2017-2018 and 2021-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
