Gray Audi A3s at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Audi A3 in our paint database was in 2006, with 2 gray paint shades named Lava Gray and Light Silver.

The latest offering of Gray on an Audi A3 is available now in the 2026 model year, with 4 shades of gray paint named Arrow Gray, Daytona Gray, Kemora Gray and Manhattan Gray.

In our database we found 70 offerings of Gray in 21 of the 21 production years of the A3 (2006-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
