Blue Audi A7s at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Audi A7 in our paint database was in 2012, with one blue paint shade named Moonlight Blue.

The latest offering of Blue on an Audi A7 is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of blue paint named Ascari Blue.

In our database we found 27 offerings of Blue in 15 of the 15 production years of the A7 (2012-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
