Silver Chrysler 200s at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Chrysler 200 in our paint database was in 2011, with one silver paint shade named Bright Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a Chrysler 200 in our paint database was in 2017, with one shade of silver paint named Billet Silver.

In our database we found 7 offerings of Silver in 7 of the 7 production years of the 200 (2011-2017). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
