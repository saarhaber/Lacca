Yellow Audi S4s at PaintRef.com


The first Yellow Audi S4 in our paint database was in 2004, with one yellow paint shade named Imola Yellow.

The last offering of Yellow on an Audi S4 in our paint database was in 2011, with one shade of yellow paint named Imola Yellow.

In our database we found 7 offerings of Yellow in 7 of the 7 production years of the S4 (2004-2008 and 2010-2011). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
