Blue Audi Q7s at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Audi Q7 in our paint database was in 2007, with 2 blue paint shades named Kobalt Blue and Mugello Blue.

The latest offering of Blue on an Audi Q7 is available now in the 2026 model year, with 2 shades of blue paint named Ascari Blue and Waitomo Blue.

In our database we found 41 offerings of Blue in 20 of the 20 production years of the Q7 (2007-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
