Silver Audi A6s at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Audi A6 in our paint database was in 2004, with one silver paint shade named Light Silver.

The latest offering of Silver on an Audi A6 is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of silver paint named Florett Silver.

In our database we found 24 offerings of Silver in 22 of the 22 production years of the A6 (2004-2019 and 2021-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
