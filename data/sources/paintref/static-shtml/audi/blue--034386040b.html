Blue Audi RS3s at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Audi RS3 in our paint database was in 2018, with one blue paint shade named Ara Blue Crystal.

The latest offering of Blue on an Audi RS3 is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of blue paint named Ascari Blue.

In our database we found 8 offerings of Blue in 8 of the 8 production years of the RS3 (2018-2024 and 2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
