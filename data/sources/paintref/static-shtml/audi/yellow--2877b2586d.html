Yellow Audi R8s at PaintRef.com


The first Yellow Audi R8 in our paint database was in 2018, with one yellow paint shade named Vegas Yellow.

The last offering of Yellow on an Audi R8 in our paint database was in 2023, with one shade of yellow paint named Vegas Yellow.

In our database we found 4 offerings of Yellow in 4 of the 4 production years of the R8 (2018 and 2021-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
