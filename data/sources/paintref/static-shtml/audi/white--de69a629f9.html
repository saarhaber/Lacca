White Audi A4s at PaintRef.com


The first White Audi A4 in our paint database was in 2004, with one white paint shade named Arctic White.

The last offering of White on an Audi A4 in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of white paint named Arkona White and Glacier White.

In our database we found 35 offerings of White in 21 of the 21 production years of the A4 (2004-2019 and 2021-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of White on an A4 in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
