Blue Audi Q5s at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Audi Q5 in our paint database was in 2010, with 2 blue paint shades named Deep Sea Blue and Strato Blue.

The latest offering of Blue on an Audi Q5 is available now in the 2026 model year, with 2 shades of blue paint named Navarra Blue and Ultra Blue.

In our database we found 36 offerings of Blue in 17 of the 17 production years of the Q5 (2010-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
