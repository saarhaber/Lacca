Blue Audi A3s at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Audi A3 in our paint database was in 2006, with 2 blue paint shades named Mauritius Blue and Moro Blue.

The latest offering of Blue on an Audi A3 is available now in the 2026 model year, with 2 shades of blue paint named Ascari Blue and Navarra Blue.

In our database we found 39 offerings of Blue in 21 of the 21 production years of the A3 (2006-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
