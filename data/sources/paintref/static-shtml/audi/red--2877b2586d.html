Red Audi R8s at PaintRef.com


The first Red Audi R8 in our paint database was in 2008, with one red paint shade named Brilliant Red.

The last offering of Red on an Audi R8 in our paint database was in 2023, with one shade of red paint named Tango Red.

In our database we found 17 offerings of Red in 15 of the 15 production years of the R8 (2008-2018 and 2020-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
