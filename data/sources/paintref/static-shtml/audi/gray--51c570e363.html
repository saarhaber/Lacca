Gray Audi Q7s at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Audi Q7 in our paint database was in 2007, with 5 gray paint shades named Condor Gray, Daytona Gray, Lava Gray, Light Silver and Quartz Gray.

The latest offering of Gray on an Audi Q7 is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of gray paint named Satellite Silver.

In our database we found 76 offerings of Gray in 20 of the 20 production years of the Q7 (2007-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
