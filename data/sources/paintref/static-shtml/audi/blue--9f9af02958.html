Blue Chrysler 200s at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Chrysler 200 in our paint database was in 2011, with 2 blue paint shades named Blackberry and Sapphire Crystal.

The last offering of Blue on a Chrysler 200 in our paint database was in 2017, with 3 shades of blue paint named Jazz Blue, Maximum Steel and Vivid Blue.

In our database we found 17 offerings of Blue in 7 of the 7 production years of the 200 (2011-2017). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a 200 in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
