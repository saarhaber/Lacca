Yellow Audi A4s at PaintRef.com


The first Yellow Audi A4 in our paint database was in 2004, with 2 yellow paint shades named Citrus Yellow and Cosmic Yellow.

The last offering of Yellow on an Audi A4 in our paint database was in 2015, with one shade of yellow paint named Imola Yellow.

In our database we found 5 offerings of Yellow in 4 of the 4 production years of the A4 (2004-2006 and 2015). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
