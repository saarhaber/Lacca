Black Audi A8s at PaintRef.com


The first Black Audi A8 in our paint database was in 2007, with 2 black paint shades named Brilliant Black and Phantom Black.

The latest offering of Black on an Audi A8 is available now in the 2026 model year, with 2 shades of black paint named Mythos Black and Sebring Black Crystal.

In our database we found 45 offerings of Black in 19 of the 19 production years of the A8 (2007-2019 and 2021-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
