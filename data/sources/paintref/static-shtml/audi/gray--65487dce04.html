Gray Audi A6s at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Audi A6 in our paint database was in 2004, with 3 gray paint shades named Crystal Blue, Dolphin Gray and Light Silver.

Gray is available now on the 2026 Audi A6, with 5 shades of gray paint named Chronos Gray, Daytona Gray, Florett Silver, Manhattan Gray and Nano Gray.

The latest offering of Gray on an Audi A6 will be available in the 2027 model year, with at least 4 shades of gray paint named Daytona Gray, Magnet Gray, Siam Beige and Typhoon Gray.

In our database we found 99 offerings of Gray in 24 of the 24 production years of the A6 (2004-2027). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
