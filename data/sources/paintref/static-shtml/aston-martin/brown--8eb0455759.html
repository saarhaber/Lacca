Brown Plymouth Valiants at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Plymouth Valiant in our paint database was in 1962, with 2 brown paint shades named Luminous Brown and Sandstone.

The last offering of Brown on a Plymouth Valiant in our paint database was in 1975, with 6 shades of brown paint named Cinnamon, Golden Fawn, Inca Gold, Sahara Beige, Sienna and Spanish Gold.

In our database we found 47 offerings of Brown in 9 of the 9 production years of the Valiant (1962-1965 and 1969-1970 and 1972-1973 and 1975). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
