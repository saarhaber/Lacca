Blue Aston Martin Vanquishs at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Aston Martin Vanquish in our paint database was in 2003, with 4 blue paint shades named Blue Sapphire, Celeste Blue, Marine Blue and Midnight Blue.

The last offering of Blue on an Aston Martin Vanquish in our paint database was in 2008, with 4 shades of blue paint named Blue Sapphire, Celeste Blue, Midnight Blue and Slate Blue.

In our database we found 25 offerings of Blue in 6 of the 6 production years of the Vanquish (2003-2008). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
