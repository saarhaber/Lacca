Gray Plymouth Valiants at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Plymouth Valiant in our paint database was in 1960, with one gray paint shade named Medallion Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a Plymouth Valiant in our paint database was in 1975, with one shade of gray paint named Silver Cloud.

In our database we found 12 offerings of Gray in 11 of the 11 production years of the Valiant (1960-1965 and 1969-1970 and 1972-1973 and 1975). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
