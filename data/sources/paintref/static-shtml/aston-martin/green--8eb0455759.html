Green Plymouth Valiants at PaintRef.com


The first Green Plymouth Valiant in our paint database was in 1960, with one green paint shade named Chrome Green.

The last offering of Green on a Plymouth Valiant in our paint database was in 1975, with 2 shades of green paint named Deep Sherwood and Frosty Green.

In our database we found 30 offerings of Green in 11 of the 11 production years of the Valiant (1960-1965 and 1969-1970 and 1972-1973 and 1975). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
