Black Cadillac CTSs at PaintRef.com


The first Black Cadillac CTS in our paint database was in 2003, with one black paint shade named Black.

The last offering of Black on a Cadillac CTS in our paint database was in 2019, with 2 shades of black paint named Black Raven and Stellar Black.

In our database we found 26 offerings of Black in 16 of the 16 production years of the CTS (2003-2012 and 2014-2019). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
