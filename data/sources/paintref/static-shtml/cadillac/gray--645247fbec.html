Gray Cadillac CTSs at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Cadillac CTS in our paint database was in 2003, with one gray paint shade named Galaxy Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a Cadillac CTS in our paint database was in 2019, with 2 shades of gray paint named Radiant Silver and Satin Steel.

In our database we found 49 offerings of Gray in 16 of the 16 production years of the CTS (2003-2012 and 2014-2019). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
