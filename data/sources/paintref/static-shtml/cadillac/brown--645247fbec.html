Brown Cadillac CTSs at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Cadillac CTS in our paint database was in 2003, with one brown paint shade named Light Bronzemist.

The last offering of Brown on a Cadillac CTS in our paint database was in 2016, with one shade of brown paint named Cocoa Bronze.

In our database we found 17 offerings of Brown in 12 of the 12 production years of the CTS (2003-2012 and 2014 and 2016). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
