Gray Cadillac ATSs at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Cadillac ATS in our paint database was in 2013, with 3 gray paint shades named Radiant Silver, Silver Coast and Thunder Gray.

The last offering of Gray on a Cadillac ATS in our paint database was in 2019, with 2 shades of gray paint named Radiant Silver and Satin Steel.

In our database we found 22 offerings of Gray in 7 of the 7 production years of the ATS (2013-2019). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
