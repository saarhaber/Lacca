Blue Cadillac Devilles at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Cadillac Deville in our paint database was in 1991, with 2 blue paint shades named Light Sapphire Blue and Medium Sapphire Blue Firemist.

The last offering of Blue on a Cadillac Deville in our paint database was in 2005, with 2 shades of blue paint named Blue Ice and Dark Ming Blue.

In our database we found 31 offerings of Blue in 15 of the 15 production years of the Deville (1991-2005). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
