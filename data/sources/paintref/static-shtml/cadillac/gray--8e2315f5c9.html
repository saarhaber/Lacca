Gray Cadillac CT4s at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Cadillac CT4 in our paint database was in 2020, with 2 gray paint shades named Satin Steel and Shadow.

The latest offering of Gray on a Cadillac CT4 is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of gray paint named Argent Silver.

In our database we found 19 offerings of Gray in 7 of the 7 production years of the CT4 (2020-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
