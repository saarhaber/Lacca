Black Cadillac CT6s at PaintRef.com


The first Black Cadillac CT6 in our paint database was in 2016, with 2 black paint shades named Black Raven and Stellar Black.

The last offering of Black on a Cadillac CT6 in our paint database was in 2020, with 2 shades of black paint named Black Raven and Stellar Black.

In our database we found 10 offerings of Black in 5 of the 5 production years of the CT6 (2016-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
