Red Cadillac CT5s at PaintRef.com


The first Red Cadillac CT5 in our paint database was in 2020, with 3 red paint shades named Garnet, Red Obsession and Velocity Red.

The latest offering of Red on a Cadillac CT5 is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of red paint named Radiant Red.

In our database we found 13 offerings of Red in 7 of the 7 production years of the CT5 (2020-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a CT5 in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
