Blue Cadillac CT6s at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Cadillac CT6 in our paint database was in 2016, with one blue paint shade named Dark Adriatic Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Cadillac CT6 in our paint database was in 2020, with one shade of blue paint named Manhattan Noir.

In our database we found 6 offerings of Blue in 5 of the 5 production years of the CT6 (2016-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a CT6 in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
