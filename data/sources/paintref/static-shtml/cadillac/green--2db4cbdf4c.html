Green Cadillac Devilles at PaintRef.com


The first Green Cadillac Deville in our paint database was in 1992, with one green paint shade named Dark Green Gray.

The last offering of Green on a Cadillac Deville in our paint database was in 2005, with one shade of green paint named Green Jewel Frost.

In our database we found 14 offerings of Green in 10 of the 10 production years of the Deville (1992-2000 and 2005). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
