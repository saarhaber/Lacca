Purple Cadillac CTSs at PaintRef.com


The first Purple Cadillac CTS in our paint database was in 2014, with one purple paint shade named Majestic Plum.

The last offering of Purple on a Cadillac CTS in our paint database was in 2017, with one shade of purple paint named Deep Amethyst.

In our database we found 3 offerings of Purple in 3 of the 3 production years of the CTS (2014-2015 and 2017). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
