Silver Cadillac Devilles at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Cadillac Deville in our paint database was in 1991, with one silver paint shade named Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a Cadillac Deville in our paint database was in 2005, with one shade of silver paint named Light Tarnished Silver.

In our database we found 13 offerings of Silver in 13 of the 13 production years of the Deville (1991-1994 and 1997-2005). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
