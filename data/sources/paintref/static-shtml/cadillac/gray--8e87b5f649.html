Gray Cadillac CT6s at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Cadillac CT6 in our paint database was in 2016, with 3 gray paint shades named Graphite, Moonstone and Radiant Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a Cadillac CT6 in our paint database was in 2020, with 3 shades of gray paint named Radiant Silver, Satin Steel and Shadow.

In our database we found 21 offerings of Gray in 5 of the 5 production years of the CT6 (2016-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on a CT6 in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
