Gray Cadillac Broughams at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Cadillac Brougham in our paint database was in 1991, with 2 gray paint shades named Gunmetal and Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a Cadillac Brougham in our paint database was in 1993, with 2 shades of gray paint named Bright Silver and Gunmetal.

In our database we found 6 offerings of Gray in 3 of the 3 production years of the Brougham (1991-1993). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
