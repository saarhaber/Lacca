Black Cadillac Devilles at PaintRef.com


The first Black Cadillac Deville in our paint database was in 1991, with 2 black paint shades named Black and Black Sapphire.

The last offering of Black on a Cadillac Deville in our paint database was in 2005, with one shade of black paint named Black.

In our database we found 18 offerings of Black in 15 of the 15 production years of the Deville (1991-2005). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
