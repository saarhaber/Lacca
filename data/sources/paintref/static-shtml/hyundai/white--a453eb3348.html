White Hyundai Tucsons at PaintRef.com


The first White Hyundai Tucson in our paint database was in 2005, with one white paint shade named Noble White.

The last offering of White on a Hyundai Tucson in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of white paint named Creamy White Pearl and Serenity White.

In our database we found 27 offerings of White in 19 of the 19 production years of the Tucson (2005-2007 and 2010-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
