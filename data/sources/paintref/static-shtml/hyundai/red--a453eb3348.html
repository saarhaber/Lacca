Red Hyundai Tucsons at PaintRef.com


The first Red Hyundai Tucson in our paint database was in 2005, with one red paint shade named Red Land.

The last offering of Red on a Hyundai Tucson in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of red paint named Ultimate Red.

In our database we found 22 offerings of Red in 20 of the 20 production years of the Tucson (2005-2009 and 2011-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
