Silver Hyundai Santa Fes at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Hyundai Santa Fe in our paint database was in 2001, with one silver paint shade named Pewter Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a Hyundai Santa Fe in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of silver paint named Shimmering Silver.

In our database we found 37 offerings of Silver in 25 of the 25 production years of the Santa Fe (2001-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
