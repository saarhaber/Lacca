Gray Hyundai Palisades at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Hyundai Palisade in our paint database was in 2020, with 3 gray paint shades named Lagoon Silver, Rainforest and Steel Graphite.

The last offering of Gray on a Hyundai Palisade in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of gray paint named Steel Graphite and Typhoon Silver.

In our database we found 16 offerings of Gray in 6 of the 6 production years of the Palisade (2020-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
