Blue Genesiss at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Genesis in our paint database was in 2009, with 2 blue paint shades named Sapphire Blue and Sterling Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Genesis in our paint database was in 2025, with 4 shades of blue paint named Bering Blue, Capri Blue, Kawah Blue and Tasman Blue.

In our database we found 53 offerings of Blue in 17 of the 17 production years of the Genesis (2009-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
