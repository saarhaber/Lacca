Brown Hyundai Tucsons at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Hyundai Tucson in our paint database was in 2005, with one brown paint shade named Golden Sand.

The last offering of Brown on a Hyundai Tucson in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of brown paint named Cashmere Bronze.

In our database we found 14 offerings of Brown in 13 of the 13 production years of the Tucson (2005 and 2010-2020 and 2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
