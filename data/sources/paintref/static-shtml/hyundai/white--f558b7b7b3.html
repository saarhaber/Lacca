White Hyundai Santa Cruzs at PaintRef.com


The first White Hyundai Santa Cruz in our paint database was in 2022, with one white paint shade named Ice White.

The last offering of White on a Hyundai Santa Cruz in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of white paint named Atlas White.

In our database we found 4 offerings of White in 4 of the 4 production years of the Santa Cruz (2022-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
