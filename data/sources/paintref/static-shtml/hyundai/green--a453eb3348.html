Green Hyundai Tucsons at PaintRef.com


The first Green Hyundai Tucson in our paint database was in 2005, with one green paint shade named New Mild Silver.

The last offering of Green on a Hyundai Tucson in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of green paint named Roockwood Green.

In our database we found 10 offerings of Green in 10 of the 10 production years of the Tucson (2005-2013 and 2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
