White Genesiss at PaintRef.com


The first White Genesis in our paint database was in 2009, with one white paint shade named White Satin Pearl.

The last offering of White on a Genesis in our paint database was in 2025, with 3 shades of white paint named Alta White, Uyuni White and Verbier White.

In our database we found 31 offerings of White in 17 of the 17 production years of the Genesis (2009-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
