Blue Hyundai Accents at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Hyundai Accent in our paint database was in 1999, with 2 blue paint shades named Cape Blue and Indigo.

The last offering of Blue on a Hyundai Accent in our paint database was in 2022, with one shade of blue paint named Admire Blue.

In our database we found 37 offerings of Blue in 24 of the 24 production years of the Accent (1999-2022). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
