Yellow Hyundai Sonatas at PaintRef.com


The first Yellow Hyundai Sonata in our paint database was in 2020, with one yellow paint shade named Glowing Yellow.

The last offering of Yellow on a Hyundai Sonata in our paint database was in 2022, with one shade of yellow paint named Glowing Yellow.

In our database we found 3 offerings of Yellow in 3 of the 3 production years of the Sonata (2020-2022). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
