White Hyundai Azeras at PaintRef.com


The first White Hyundai Azera in our paint database was in 2006, with one white paint shade named Pure Pearl White.

The last offering of White on a Hyundai Azera in our paint database was in 2017, with 2 shades of white paint named Diamond White and Hyper White.

In our database we found 14 offerings of White in 12 of the 12 production years of the Azera (2006-2017). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
