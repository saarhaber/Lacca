Silver Hyundai Azeras at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Hyundai Azera in our paint database was in 2006, with one silver paint shade named Sleek Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a Hyundai Azera in our paint database was in 2018, with one shade of silver paint named Ion Silver.

In our database we found 18 offerings of Silver in 13 of the 13 production years of the Azera (2006-2018). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
