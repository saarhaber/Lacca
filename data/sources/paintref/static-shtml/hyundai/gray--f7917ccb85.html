Gray Hyundai Ioniqs at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Hyundai Ioniq in our paint database was in 2017, with 2 gray paint shades named Dark Gray and Symphony Air Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a Hyundai Ioniq in our paint database was in 2025, with 5 shades of gray paint named Celadon Gray, Curated Silver, Cyber Gray, Ecotronic Gray and Transmission Blue.

In our database we found 24 offerings of Gray in 9 of the 9 production years of the Ioniq (2017-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
