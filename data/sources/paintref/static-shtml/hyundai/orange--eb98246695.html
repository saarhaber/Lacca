Orange Hyundai Santa Fes at PaintRef.com


The first Orange Hyundai Santa Fe in our paint database was in 2001, with one orange paint shade named Copper Orange.

The last offering of Orange on a Hyundai Santa Fe in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of orange paint named Terracotta Orage.

In our database we found 13 offerings of Orange in 13 of the 13 production years of the Santa Fe (2001 and 2003-2004 and 2013-2020 and 2024-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
