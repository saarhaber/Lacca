Gray Hyundai Santa Fes at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Hyundai Santa Fe in our paint database was in 2001, with one gray paint shade named Pewter Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a Hyundai Santa Fe in our paint database was in 2025, with 4 shades of gray paint named Ecotronic Gray, Hampton Gray, Roockwood Green and Shimmering Silver.

In our database we found 91 offerings of Gray in 25 of the 25 production years of the Santa Fe (2001-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
