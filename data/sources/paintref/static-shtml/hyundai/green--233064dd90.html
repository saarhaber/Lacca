Green Hyundai Accents at PaintRef.com


The first Green Hyundai Accent in our paint database was in 1999, with 2 green paint shades named Jungle Green and Madras Green.

The last offering of Green on a Hyundai Accent in our paint database was in 2014, with one shade of green paint named Electrolyte Green.

In our database we found 15 offerings of Green in 14 of the 14 production years of the Accent (1999-2001 and 2003-2005 and 2007-2014). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
