White Hyundai Sonatas at PaintRef.com


The first White Hyundai Sonata in our paint database was in 1999, with 2 white paint shades named Noble White and White Pearl.

The last offering of White on a Hyundai Sonata in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of white paint named Serenity White.

In our database we found 48 offerings of White in 27 of the 27 production years of the Sonata (1999-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of White on a Sonata in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
