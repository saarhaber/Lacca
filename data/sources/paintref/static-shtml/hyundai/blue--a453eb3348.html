Blue Hyundai Tucsons at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Hyundai Tucson in our paint database was in 2005, with 2 blue paint shades named Aqua Silver and Marine Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Hyundai Tucson in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of blue paint named Atlantis Blue and Deep Sea.

In our database we found 40 offerings of Blue in 21 of the 21 production years of the Tucson (2005-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
