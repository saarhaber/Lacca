Silver Genesiss at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Genesis in our paint database was in 2010, with one silver paint shade named Sleek Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a Genesis in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of silver paint named Savile Silver.

In our database we found 27 offerings of Silver in 16 of the 16 production years of the Genesis (2010-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
