Brown Hyundai Santa Fes at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Hyundai Santa Fe in our paint database was in 2001, with one brown paint shade named Sandstone Beige.

The last offering of Brown on a Hyundai Santa Fe in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of brown paint named Earthy Brass and Terracotta Orage.

In our database we found 38 offerings of Brown in 21 of the 21 production years of the Santa Fe (2001-2009 and 2011-2020 and 2024-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
