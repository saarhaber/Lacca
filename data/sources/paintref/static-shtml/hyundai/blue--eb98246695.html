Blue Hyundai Santa Fes at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Hyundai Santa Fe in our paint database was in 2001, with 2 blue paint shades named Crystal Blue and Pine Green.

The last offering of Blue on a Hyundai Santa Fe in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of blue paint named Atlantis Blue.

In our database we found 45 offerings of Blue in 23 of the 23 production years of the Santa Fe (2001-2009 and 2011-2023 and 2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
