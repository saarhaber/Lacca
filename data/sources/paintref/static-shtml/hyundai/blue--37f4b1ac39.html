Blue Hyundai Sonatas at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Hyundai Sonata in our paint database was in 1999, with one blue paint shade named Napoli Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Hyundai Sonata in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of blue paint named Carbon Blue and Transmission Blue.

In our database we found 71 offerings of Blue in 27 of the 27 production years of the Sonata (1999-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
