Silver Hyundai Konas at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Hyundai Kona in our paint database was in 2018, with one silver paint shade named Sonic Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a Hyundai Kona in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of silver paint named Cyber Gray.

In our database we found 8 offerings of Silver in 8 of the 8 production years of the Kona (2018-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
