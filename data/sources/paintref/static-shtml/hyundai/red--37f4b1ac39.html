Red Hyundai Sonatas at PaintRef.com


The first Red Hyundai Sonata in our paint database was in 1999, with one red paint shade named Canton Cherry.

The last offering of Red on a Hyundai Sonata in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of red paint named Ultimate Red.

In our database we found 36 offerings of Red in 26 of the 26 production years of the Sonata (1999-2005 and 2007-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
