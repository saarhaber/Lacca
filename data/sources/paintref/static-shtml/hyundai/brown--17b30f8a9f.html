Brown Genesiss at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Genesis in our paint database was in 2009, with one brown paint shade named Champagne Beige.

The last offering of Brown on a Genesis in our paint database was in 2024, with one shade of brown paint named Atacma Copper.

In our database we found 17 offerings of Brown in 13 of the 13 production years of the Genesis (2009-2013 and 2015-2019 and 2022-2024). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
