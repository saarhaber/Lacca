Red Hyundai Konas at PaintRef.com


The first Red Hyundai Kona in our paint database was in 2018, with one red paint shade named Pulse Red.

The last offering of Red on a Hyundai Kona in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of red paint named Ultimate Red.

In our database we found 11 offerings of Red in 8 of the 8 production years of the Kona (2018-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
