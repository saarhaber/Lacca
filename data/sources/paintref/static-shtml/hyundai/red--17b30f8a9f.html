Red Genesiss at PaintRef.com


The first Red Genesis in our paint database was in 2009, with one red paint shade named Cabernet Red.

The last offering of Red on a Genesis in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of red paint named Cavendish Red and Mauna Red.

In our database we found 39 offerings of Red in 17 of the 17 production years of the Genesis (2009-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
