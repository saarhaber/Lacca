White Hyundai Elantras at PaintRef.com


The first White Hyundai Elantra in our paint database was in 1999, with one white paint shade named Noble White.

The last offering of White on a Hyundai Elantra in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of white paint named Atlas White and Serenity White.

In our database we found 46 offerings of White in 27 of the 27 production years of the Elantra (1999-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
