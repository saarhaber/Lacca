Black Hyundai Santa Fes at PaintRef.com


The first Black Hyundai Santa Fe in our paint database was in 2001, with one black paint shade named Black.

The last offering of Black on a Hyundai Santa Fe in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of black paint named Phantom Black.

In our database we found 36 offerings of Black in 24 of the 24 production years of the Santa Fe (2001-2009 and 2011-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
