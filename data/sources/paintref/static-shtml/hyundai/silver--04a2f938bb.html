Silver Hyundai Elantras at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Hyundai Elantra in our paint database was in 1999, with one silver paint shade named Bright Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a Hyundai Elantra in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of silver paint named Cyber Gray.

In our database we found 48 offerings of Silver in 25 of the 25 production years of the Elantra (1999 and 2001-2023 and 2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
