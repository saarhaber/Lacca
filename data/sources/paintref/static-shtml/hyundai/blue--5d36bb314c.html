Blue Hyundai Konas at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Hyundai Kona in our paint database was in 2018, with 2 blue paint shades named Blue Lagoon and Ceramic Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Hyundai Kona in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of blue paint named Denim Blue and Meta Blue.

In our database we found 17 offerings of Blue in 8 of the 8 production years of the Kona (2018-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
