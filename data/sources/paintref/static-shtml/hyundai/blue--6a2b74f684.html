Blue Hyundai Tiburons at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Hyundai Tiburon in our paint database was in 1999, with 2 blue paint shades named Cape Blue and Cobalt Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Hyundai Tiburon in our paint database was in 2008, with 2 shades of blue paint named Nocturnal Blue and Vivid Blue.

In our database we found 18 offerings of Blue in 9 of the 9 production years of the Tiburon (1999-2001 and 2003-2008). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
