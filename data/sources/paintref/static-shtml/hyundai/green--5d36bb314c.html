Green Hyundai Konas at PaintRef.com


The first Green Hyundai Kona in our paint database was in 2018, with one green paint shade named Lime Twist.

The last offering of Green on a Hyundai Kona in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of green paint named Mirage Green and Neoteric Yellow.

In our database we found 9 offerings of Green in 7 of the 7 production years of the Kona (2018-2020 and 2022-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
