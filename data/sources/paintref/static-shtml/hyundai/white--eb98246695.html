White Hyundai Santa Fes at PaintRef.com


The first White Hyundai Santa Fe in our paint database was in 2001, with one white paint shade named Alpine Noble White.

The last offering of White on a Hyundai Santa Fe in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of white paint named Serenity White.

In our database we found 37 offerings of White in 25 of the 25 production years of the Santa Fe (2001-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
