Blue Hyundai Elantras at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Hyundai Elantra in our paint database was in 1999, with 2 blue paint shades named Cape Blue and Cobalt Blue.

The last offering of Blue on a Hyundai Elantra in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of blue paint named Intense Blue and Performance Blue.

In our database we found 60 offerings of Blue in 27 of the 27 production years of the Elantra (1999-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
