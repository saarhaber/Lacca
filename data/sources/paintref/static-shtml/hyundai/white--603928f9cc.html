White Hyundai Nexos at PaintRef.com


The first White Hyundai Nexo in our paint database was in 2019, with one white paint shade named White Pearl.

The last offering of White on a Hyundai Nexo in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of white paint named Creamy White Pearl.

In our database we found 7 offerings of White in 7 of the 7 production years of the Nexo (2019-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
