Red Hyundai Tiburons at PaintRef.com


The first Red Hyundai Tiburon in our paint database was in 1999, with 3 red paint shades named Cardinal Red, Wild Rose and Wildberry Red.

The last offering of Red on a Hyundai Tiburon in our paint database was in 2008, with one shade of red paint named Redfire.

In our database we found 15 offerings of Red in 9 of the 9 production years of the Tiburon (1999-2001 and 2003-2008). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
