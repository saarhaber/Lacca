Red Hyundai Accents at PaintRef.com


The first Red Hyundai Accent in our paint database was in 1999, with one red paint shade named Cherry Red.

The last offering of Red on a Hyundai Accent in our paint database was in 2022, with one shade of red paint named Pomegranate Red.

In our database we found 38 offerings of Red in 24 of the 24 production years of the Accent (1999-2022). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
