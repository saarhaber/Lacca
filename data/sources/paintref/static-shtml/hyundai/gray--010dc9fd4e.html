Gray Hyundai Equuss at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Hyundai Equus in our paint database was in 2011, with 2 gray paint shades named Granite Gray and Platinum.

The last offering of Gray on a Hyundai Equus in our paint database was in 2016, with 3 shades of gray paint named Night Shadow, Parisian Gray and Santiago Silver.

In our database we found 15 offerings of Gray in 6 of the 6 production years of the Equus (2011-2016). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
