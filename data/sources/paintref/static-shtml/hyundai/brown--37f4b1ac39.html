Brown Hyundai Sonatas at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Hyundai Sonata in our paint database was in 2002, with one brown paint shade named Light Beige.

The last offering of Brown on a Hyundai Sonata in our paint database was in 2017, with 2 shades of brown paint named Dark Truffle and Mineral Beige.

In our database we found 28 offerings of Brown in 14 of the 14 production years of the Sonata (2002-2011 and 2014-2017). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
