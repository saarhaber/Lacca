Gray Hyundai Velosters at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Hyundai Veloster in our paint database was in 2012, with 2 gray paint shades named Ironman Silver and Triathlon Gray.

The last offering of Gray on a Hyundai Veloster in our paint database was in 2021, with 2 shades of gray paint named Sonic Silver and Thunder Gray.

In our database we found 31 offerings of Gray in 10 of the 10 production years of the Veloster (2012-2021). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
