Black Hyundai Azeras at PaintRef.com


The first Black Hyundai Azera in our paint database was in 2006, with one black paint shade named Ebony Black.

The last offering of Black on a Hyundai Azera in our paint database was in 2018, with one shade of black paint named Nocturne Black.

In our database we found 14 offerings of Black in 13 of the 13 production years of the Azera (2006-2018). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
