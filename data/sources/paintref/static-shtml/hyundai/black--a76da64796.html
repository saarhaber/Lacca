Black Hyundai Entourages at PaintRef.com


The first Black Hyundai Entourage in our paint database was in 2007, with one black paint shade named Galaxy Black.

The last offering of Black on a Hyundai Entourage in our paint database was in 2009, with one shade of black paint named Galaxy Black.

In our database we found 3 offerings of Black in 3 of the 3 production years of the Entourage (2007-2009). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
