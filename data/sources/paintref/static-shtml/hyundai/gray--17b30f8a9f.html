Gray Genesiss at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Genesis in our paint database was in 2009, with 2 gray paint shades named Platinum and Titanium Gray.

The last offering of Gray on a Genesis in our paint database was in 2025, with 6 shades of gray paint named Makalu Gray, Makalu Gray Matte, Matterhorn White, Savile Silver, Storr Green and Vatna Gray.

In our database we found 80 offerings of Gray in 17 of the 17 production years of the Genesis (2009-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
