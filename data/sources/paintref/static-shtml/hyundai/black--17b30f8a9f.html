Black Genesiss at PaintRef.com


The first Black Genesis in our paint database was in 2009, with one black paint shade named Phantom Black.

The last offering of Black on a Genesis in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of black paint named Maui Black and Vik Black.

In our database we found 26 offerings of Black in 17 of the 17 production years of the Genesis (2009-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
