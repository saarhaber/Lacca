Red Hyundai Elantras at PaintRef.com


The first Red Hyundai Elantra in our paint database was in 1999, with 3 red paint shades named Cardinal Red, Wild Rose and Wildberry Red.

The last offering of Red on a Hyundai Elantra in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of red paint named Ultimate Red.

In our database we found 55 offerings of Red in 27 of the 27 production years of the Elantra (1999-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
