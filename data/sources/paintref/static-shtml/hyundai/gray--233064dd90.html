Gray Hyundai Accents at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Hyundai Accent in our paint database was in 1999, with one gray paint shade named Cool Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a Hyundai Accent in our paint database was in 2022, with 2 shades of gray paint named Forge Gray and Olympus Silver.

In our database we found 54 offerings of Gray in 24 of the 24 production years of the Accent (1999-2022). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
