Gray Hyundai Konas at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Hyundai Kona in our paint database was in 2018, with 2 gray paint shades named Sonic Silver and Thunder Gray.

The last offering of Gray on a Hyundai Kona in our paint database was in 2025, with 3 shades of gray paint named Cyber Gray, Ecotronic Gray and Mirage Green.

In our database we found 27 offerings of Gray in 8 of the 8 production years of the Kona (2018-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
