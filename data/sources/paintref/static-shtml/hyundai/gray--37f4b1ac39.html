Gray Hyundai Sonatas at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Hyundai Sonata in our paint database was in 1999, with 2 gray paint shades named Bright Silver and Urban Gray.

The last offering of Gray on a Hyundai Sonata in our paint database was in 2025, with 3 shades of gray paint named Aero Silver, Portofino Gray and Transmission Blue.

In our database we found 88 offerings of Gray in 27 of the 27 production years of the Sonata (1999-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
