Red Hyundai Ioniqs at PaintRef.com


The first Red Hyundai Ioniq in our paint database was in 2018, with 2 red paint shades named Fiery Red and Phoenix Orange.

The last offering of Red on a Hyundai Ioniq in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of red paint named Ultimate Red.

In our database we found 9 offerings of Red in 7 of the 7 production years of the Ioniq (2018-2022 and 2024-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
