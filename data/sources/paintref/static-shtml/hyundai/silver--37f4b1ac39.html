Silver Hyundai Sonatas at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Hyundai Sonata in our paint database was in 1999, with one silver paint shade named Bright Silver.

The last offering of Silver on a Hyundai Sonata in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of silver paint named Aero Silver.

In our database we found 53 offerings of Silver in 27 of the 27 production years of the Sonata (1999-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
