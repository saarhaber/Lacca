Gray Hyundai XG350s at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Hyundai XG350 in our paint database was in 2002, with 2 gray paint shades named Bright Silver and Urban Gray.

The last offering of Gray on a Hyundai XG350 in our paint database was in 2005, with 2 shades of gray paint named Charming Gray and Sleek Silver.

In our database we found 8 offerings of Gray in 4 of the 4 production years of the XG350 (2002-2005). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
