Gray Hyundai Tucsons at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Hyundai Tucson in our paint database was in 2005, with 2 gray paint shades named Technical Gray and Warm Silver.

The last offering of Gray on a Hyundai Tucson in our paint database was in 2025, with 7 shades of gray paint named Amazon Gray, Ecotronic Gray, Hampton Gray, Roockwood Green, Shimmering Silver, Titan Grey and Titanium Grey.

In our database we found 58 offerings of Gray in 21 of the 21 production years of the Tucson (2005-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
