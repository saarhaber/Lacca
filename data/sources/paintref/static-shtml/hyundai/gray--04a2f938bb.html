Gray Hyundai Elantras at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Hyundai Elantra in our paint database was in 1999, with 2 gray paint shades named Bright Silver and Urban Gray.

The last offering of Gray on a Hyundai Elantra in our paint database was in 2025, with 4 shades of gray paint named Amazon Gray, Cyber Gray, Ecotronic Gray and Fluid Metal.

In our database we found 101 offerings of Gray in 27 of the 27 production years of the Elantra (1999-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
