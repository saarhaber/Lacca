Silver Acura TSXs at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Acura TSX in our paint database was in 2004, with 2 silver paint shades named Meteor Silver and Satin Silver.

The last offering of Silver on an Acura TSX in our paint database was in 2014, with 2 shades of silver paint named Alabaster Silver and Forged Silver.

In our database we found 17 offerings of Silver in 11 of the 11 production years of the TSX (2004-2014). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
