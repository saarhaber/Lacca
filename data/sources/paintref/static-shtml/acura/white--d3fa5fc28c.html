White Acura TLs at PaintRef.com


The first White Acura TL in our paint database was in 1998, with one white paint shade named Cayman White.

The last offering of White on an Acura TL in our paint database was in 2014, with one shade of white paint named White Orchid.

In our database we found 21 offerings of White in 17 of the 17 production years of the TL (1998-2014). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of White on a TL in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
