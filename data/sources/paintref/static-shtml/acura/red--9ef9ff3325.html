Red Acura TLXs at PaintRef.com


The first Red Acura TLX in our paint database was in 2015, with one red paint shade named Basque Red.

The last offering of Red on an Acura TLX in our paint database was in 2023, with 2 shades of red paint named Curva Red and Performance Red.

In our database we found 11 offerings of Red in 9 of the 9 production years of the TLX (2015-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on a TLX in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
