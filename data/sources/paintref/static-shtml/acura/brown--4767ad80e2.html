Brown Acura RLs at PaintRef.com


The first Brown Acura RL in our paint database was in 1999, with one brown paint shade named Golden Opal.

The last offering of Brown on an Acura RL in our paint database was in 2010, with one shade of brown paint named Tuscan Beige.

In our database we found 15 offerings of Brown in 12 of the 12 production years of the RL (1999-2010). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Brown on an RL in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
