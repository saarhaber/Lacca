Gray Acura TLs at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Acura TL in our paint database was in 1998, with one gray paint shade named Granite Silver.

The last offering of Gray on an Acura TL in our paint database was in 2014, with 2 shades of gray paint named Alabaster Silver and Graphite Luster.

In our database we found 33 offerings of Gray in 17 of the 17 production years of the TL (1998-2014). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on a TL in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
