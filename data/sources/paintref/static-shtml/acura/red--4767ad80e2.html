Red Acura RLs at PaintRef.com


The first Red Acura RL in our paint database was in 1998, with one red paint shade named New Vivid Red.

The last offering of Red on an Acura RL in our paint database was in 2012, with one shade of red paint named Pomegranate.

In our database we found 12 offerings of Red in 12 of the 12 production years of the RL (1998-2002 and 2005-2009 and 2011-2012). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Red on an RL in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
