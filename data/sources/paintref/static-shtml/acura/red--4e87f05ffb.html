Red Acura RDXs at PaintRef.com


The first Red Acura RDX in our paint database was in 2007, with one red paint shade named Moroccan Red.

The last offering of Red on an Acura RDX in our paint database was in 2025, with one shade of red paint named Performance Red.

In our database we found 23 offerings of Red in 19 of the 19 production years of the RDX (2007-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
