Blue Acura RDXs at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Acura RDX in our paint database was in 2007, with 2 blue paint shades named Nighthawk Black and Royal Blue.

The last offering of Blue on an Acura RDX in our paint database was in 2025, with 2 shades of blue paint named Apex Blue and Canyon River Blue.

In our database we found 20 offerings of Blue in 15 of the 15 production years of the RDX (2007-2011 and 2016-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on an RDX in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
