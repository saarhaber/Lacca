Gray Acura RLXs at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Acura RLX in our paint database was in 2014, with 4 gray paint shades named Alabaster Silver, Forged Silver, Gilded Pewter and Graphite Luster.

The last offering of Gray on an Acura RLX in our paint database was in 2020, with 3 shades of gray paint named Gilded Pewter, Lunar Silver and Modern Steel.

In our database we found 25 offerings of Gray in 7 of the 7 production years of the RLX (2014-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on an RLX in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
