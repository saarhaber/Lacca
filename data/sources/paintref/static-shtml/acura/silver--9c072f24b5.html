Silver Acura MDXs at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Acura MDX in our paint database was in 2001, with one silver paint shade named Starlight Silver.

The latest offering of Silver on an Acura MDX is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of silver paint named Solar Silver.

In our database we found 25 offerings of Silver in 25 of the 25 production years of the MDX (2001-2020 and 2022-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
