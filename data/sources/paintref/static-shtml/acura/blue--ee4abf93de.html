Blue Acura ILXs at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Acura ILX in our paint database was in 2013, with one blue paint shade named Fathom Blue.

The last offering of Blue on an Acura ILX in our paint database was in 2021, with one shade of blue paint named Apex Blue.

In our database we found 9 offerings of Blue in 9 of the 9 production years of the ILX (2013-2021). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
