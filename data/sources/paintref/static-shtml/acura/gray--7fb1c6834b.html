Gray Integras at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Integra in our paint database was in 1994, with 2 gray paint shades named Stealth Gray and Thunder Gray.

The latest offering of Gray on an Integra is available now in the 2026 model year, with 2 shades of gray paint named Solar Silver and Urban Gray.

In our database we found 29 offerings of Gray in 13 of the 13 production years of the Integra (1994-2002 and 2023-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
