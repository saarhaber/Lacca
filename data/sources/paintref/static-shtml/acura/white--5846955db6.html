White Acura Legends at PaintRef.com


The first White Acura Legend in our paint database was in 1994, with 2 white paint shades named Sirius White and Taffeta White.

The last offering of White on an Acura Legend in our paint database was in 1997, with one shade of white paint named Cayman White.

In our database we found 8 offerings of White in 4 of the 4 production years of the Legend (1994-1997). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of White on a Legend in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
