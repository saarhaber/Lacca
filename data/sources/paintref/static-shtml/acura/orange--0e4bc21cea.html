Orange Acura NSXs at PaintRef.com


The first Orange Acura NSX in our paint database was in 1997, with one orange paint shade named Imora Orange.

The last offering of Orange on an Acura NSX in our paint database was in 2023, with one shade of orange paint named Thermal Orange.

In our database we found 10 offerings of Orange in 10 of the 10 production years of the NSX (1997 and 2002-2005 and 2019-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
