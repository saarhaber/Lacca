Blue Acura NSXs at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Acura NSX in our paint database was in 1994, with one blue paint shade named Brookland Green.

The last offering of Blue on an Acura NSX in our paint database was in 2023, with 2 shades of blue paint named Long Beach Blue and Nouvelle Blue.

In our database we found 25 offerings of Blue in 19 of the 19 production years of the NSX (1994-2005 and 2017-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
