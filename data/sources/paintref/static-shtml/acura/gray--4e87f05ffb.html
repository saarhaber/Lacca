Gray Acura RDXs at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Acura RDX in our paint database was in 2007, with 2 gray paint shades named Alabaster Silver and Graphite.

The last offering of Gray on an Acura RDX in our paint database was in 2025, with 3 shades of gray paint named Liquid Carbon, Solar Silver and Urban Gray.

In our database we found 48 offerings of Gray in 19 of the 19 production years of the RDX (2007-2025). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on an RDX in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
