Gray Acura CLs at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Acura CL in our paint database was in 1998, with one gray paint shade named Primrose Mist.

The last offering of Gray on an Acura CL in our paint database was in 2003, with 2 shades of gray paint named Anthracite Gray and Satin Silver.

In our database we found 6 offerings of Gray in 5 of the 5 production years of the CL (1998-1999 and 2001-2003). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on a CL in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
