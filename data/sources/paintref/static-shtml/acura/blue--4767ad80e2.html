Blue Acura RLs at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Acura RL in our paint database was in 1998, with 2 blue paint shades named Crystal Blue and Juniper Green.

The last offering of Blue on an Acura RL in our paint database was in 2010, with one shade of blue paint named Opulent Blue.

In our database we found 26 offerings of Blue in 13 of the 13 production years of the RL (1998-2010). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on an RL in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
