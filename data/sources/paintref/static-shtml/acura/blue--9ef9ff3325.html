Blue Acura TLXs at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Acura TLX in our paint database was in 2015, with one blue paint shade named Obsidian Blue.

The last offering of Blue on an Acura TLX in our paint database was in 2024, with one shade of blue paint named Apex Blue.

In our database we found 15 offerings of Blue in 10 of the 10 production years of the TLX (2015-2024). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Blue on a TLX in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
