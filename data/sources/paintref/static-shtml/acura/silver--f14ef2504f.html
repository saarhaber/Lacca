Silver Acura RLXs at PaintRef.com


The first Silver Acura RLX in our paint database was in 2014, with 2 silver paint shades named Alabaster Silver and Forged Silver.

The last offering of Silver on an Acura RLX in our paint database was in 2020, with one shade of silver paint named Lunar Silver.

In our database we found 10 offerings of Silver in 7 of the 7 production years of the RLX (2014-2020). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
