Yellow Acura NSXs at PaintRef.com


The first Yellow Acura NSX in our paint database was in 1997, with one yellow paint shade named Indy Yellow.

The last offering of Yellow on an Acura NSX in our paint database was in 2023, with one shade of yellow paint named Indy Yellow.

In our database we found 13 offerings of Yellow in 13 of the 13 production years of the NSX (1997-2005 and 2020-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
