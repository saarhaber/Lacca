Red Acura MDXs at PaintRef.com


The first Red Acura MDX in our paint database was in 2003, with one red paint shade named Red Rock.

The latest offering of Red on an Acura MDX is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of red paint named Performance Red.

In our database we found 24 offerings of Red in 23 of the 23 production years of the MDX (2003-2020 and 2022-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
