Gray Acura RLs at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Acura RL in our paint database was in 1998, with one gray paint shade named Flint Gray.

The last offering of Gray on an Acura RL in our paint database was in 2012, with 3 shades of gray paint named Forged Silver, Graphite Luster and Platinum Frost.

In our database we found 36 offerings of Gray in 15 of the 15 production years of the RL (1998-2012). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on an RL in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
