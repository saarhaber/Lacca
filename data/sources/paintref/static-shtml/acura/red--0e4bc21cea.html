Red Acura NSXs at PaintRef.com


The first Red Acura NSX in our paint database was in 1994, with one red paint shade named Formula Red.

The last offering of Red on an Acura NSX in our paint database was in 2023, with 2 shades of red paint named Curva Red and Valencia Red.

In our database we found 26 offerings of Red in 18 of the 18 production years of the NSX (1994-2003 and 2005 and 2017-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
