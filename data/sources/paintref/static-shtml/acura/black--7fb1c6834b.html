Black Integras at PaintRef.com


The first Black Integra in our paint database was in 1994, with one black paint shade named Granada Black.

The latest offering of Black on an Integra is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of black paint named Majestic Black.

In our database we found 11 offerings of Black in 11 of the 11 production years of the Integra (1994-2000 and 2023-2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Black on an Integra in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
