Black Acura NSXs at PaintRef.com


The first Black Acura NSX in our paint database was in 1994, with one black paint shade named Berlina Black.

The last offering of Black on an Acura NSX in our paint database was in 2023, with one shade of black paint named Berlina Black.

In our database we found 19 offerings of Black in 19 of the 19 production years of the NSX (1994-2005 and 2017-2023). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
