Gray Acura Legends at PaintRef.com


The first Gray Acura Legend in our paint database was in 1994, with 3 gray paint shades named Canterberry Green, Desert Mist and Vineyard Gray.

The last offering of Gray on an Acura Legend in our paint database was in 1997, with 6 shades of gray paint named Desert Mist, Devon Slate, Granite Silver, Kaiser Silver, New Blue Silver Gray and Silver Stone.

In our database we found 14 offerings of Gray in 4 of the 4 production years of the Legend (1994-1997). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow. Below is a list of all offerings of Gray on a Legend in our database with examples where we have found them. Click on the example or manufacturing code for more information.
