Green Acura CLs at PaintRef.com


The first Green Acura CL in our paint database was in 1998, with one green paint shade named Dark Blue Green.

The last offering of Green on an Acura CL in our paint database was in 2003, with one shade of green paint named Noble Green.

In our database we found 6 offerings of Green in 5 of the 5 production years of the CL (1998-1999 and 2001-2003). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
