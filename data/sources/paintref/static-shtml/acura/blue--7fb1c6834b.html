Blue Integras at PaintRef.com


The first Blue Integra in our paint database was in 1994, with 2 blue paint shades named Isle Green and Paradise Blue Green.

The latest offering of Blue on an Integra is available now in the 2026 model year, with one shade of blue paint named Double Apex Blue.

In our database we found 19 offerings of Blue in 11 of the 11 production years of the Integra (1994-2001 and 2023-2024 and 2026). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
