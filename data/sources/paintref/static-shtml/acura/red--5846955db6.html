Red Acura Legends at PaintRef.com


The first Red Acura Legend in our paint database was in 1994, with one red paint shade named Milano Red.

The last offering of Red on an Acura Legend in our paint database was in 1997, with one shade of red paint named Garnet Red.

In our database we found 6 offerings of Red in 4 of the 4 production years of the Legend (1994-1997). We have excluded all yellow greens and most golds from this list but included all gold yellow paints as originally named; light yellowish creams, tans, and beiges; and those paints are very obviously yellow.
